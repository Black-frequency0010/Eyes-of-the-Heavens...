SEASON 5

EPISODE 7: THE SELECTED PATH

---

SCENE 1 — THE WORLD FEELS DIFFERENT

The convergence continues.

The world is calmer now.

Too calm.

No contradictions.

No instability.

No uncertainty.

Reality feels singular.

Kairo stands silently.

Watching.

Listening.

Something about it feels wrong.

---

SCENE 2 — THE LOSS OF POSSIBILITY

Across the world—

people stop questioning their memories.

History becomes fixed.

Events become absolute.

Alternative outcomes vanish.

Kaze studies the changes.

KAZE:

"...It's happening faster now."

A pause.

KAZE:

"The selection process has accelerated."

---

SCENE 3 — REIKA'S FEAR

Reika walks through the city.

Everything appears normal.

But that is exactly what terrifies her.

REIKA:

"...It's too consistent."

Akari looks at her.

AKARI:

"What does that mean?"

Reika answers immediately.

REIKA:

"It means reality already made a decision."

Silence.

---

SCENE 4 — THE UNKNOWN BOY WATCHES

The Unknown Boy remains still.

He no longer moves.

He no longer reacts.

He simply watches.

As if he already knows what comes next.

Kairo notices.

And for the first time—

he feels afraid of him.

---

SCENE 5 — THE FIFTH LAW

Deep inside the origin fragments—

Kaze finds another record.

This one is sealed.

Older than every law before it.

He opens it.

A single line appears:

«“WHEN CONVERGENCE REACHES COMPLETION, ALL NON-SELECTED EXISTENCE SHALL CEASE.”»

Silence.

Kaze steps backward.

KAZE:

"...No."

A pause.

KAZE:

"...That's impossible."

---

SCENE 6 — KAIRO HEARS THE TRUTH

Kaze shows the fragment to Kairo and Reika.

Neither speaks immediately.

Because both understand the implication.

KAIRO:

"...If reality chooses one version..."

A pause.

KAIRO:

"...then every other version dies."

Silence.

---

SCENE 7 — THE FIRST GLIMPSE OF THE END

For a brief moment—

all three witness something.

A vision.

Not of the past.

Not of the present.

The future.

A reality where the convergence is complete.

A reality where only one truth remains.

Then the vision disappears.

Reika begins shaking.

REIKA:

"...I saw it."

KAIRO:

"So did I."

---

FINAL SCENE — THE WARNING

The sky changes again.

A final message appears.

"CONVERGENCE PHASE: 50% COMPLETE"

A pause.

Then:

"NON-SELECTED REALITY TERMINATION PREPARATION ACTIVE"

Silence.

No one moves.

No one speaks.

Because now they understand.

The correction is no longer deciding what stays.

It is preparing to destroy everything that doesn't.

CUT TO BLACK.

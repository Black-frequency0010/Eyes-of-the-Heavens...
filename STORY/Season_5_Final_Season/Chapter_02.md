SEASON 5

EPISODE 2: THE FIRST LOSS

---

SCENE 1 — AFTER THE MESSAGE

Silence remains after the sky message disappears.

"THE RETURN HAS BEEN ACCEPTED."

"CORRECTION PHASE: BEGINNING."

No one speaks for a while.

Kairo is still standing.

Reika is watching the sky.

Kaze is staring at the ground.

Then—

Kairo suddenly pauses.

Something is missing.

Not visually.

Internally.

He reaches into his memory.

And finds nothing.

---

SCENE 2 — THE FIRST DISAPPEARANCE

Kairo turns sharply.

KAIRO:

"...Someone is gone."

Reika looks at him.

REIKA:

"Who?"

Kairo tries to respond.

But stops.

His expression changes.

Confusion.

Then fear.

KAIRO:

"I don’t… remember."

Silence.

Kaze immediately reacts.

KAZE:

"It started."

---

SCENE 3 — MEMORY BREAKDOWN

Kaze opens a Tenshirō archive node.

Records are shifting again.

But now faster.

Entire names flicker in and out of existence.

Kaze tries to stabilize one file.

It fails instantly.

KAZE:

"It's not targeting records."

A pause.

KAZE:

"It's targeting continuity."

---

SCENE 4 — REIKA’S SENSATION

Reika grips her head.

Something is wrong.

Not pain.

Absence.

Like something important was removed from inside her awareness.

She speaks slowly.

REIKA:

"There was someone with us… wasn’t there?"

Silence.

No answer comes.

Not even from herself.

---

SCENE 5 — THE UNKNOWN BOY OBSERVATION

Far away.

The Unknown Boy continues walking.

Calm.

Unchanging.

But reality reacts to every step.

Behind him—

space subtly reorganizes.

Not destruction.

Revision.

A version of the world briefly appears where he never walked there.

Then it disappears.

---

SCENE 6 — FIRST CONFIRMED LOSS

Kairo looks at Reika.

KAIRO:

"I had something I needed to remember."

A pause.

KAIRO:

"But I can’t hold it anymore."

Reika nods slowly.

REIKA:

"It’s not just memory."

REIKA:

"It’s meaning."

Kaze interrupts.

KAZE:

"No."

A pause.

KAZE:

"It’s identity-level removal."

Silence.

---

SCENE 7 — REALITY CORRECTION EXPANDS

A distant city flickers.

People pause mid-action.

Then continue as if nothing happened.

But subtle changes occur:

- relationships shift slightly
- objects lose personal meaning
- names feel unfamiliar even to their owners

The correction is spreading.

Quietly.

Systematically.

---

SCENE 8 — THE SECOND MESSAGE

The sky returns.

A new line appears.

"CORRECTION SUCCESS: FIRST VARIABLE REMOVED."

Kairo looks up.

KAIRO:

"...First variable?"

Kaze’s eyes widen.

KAZE:

"It’s not random."

A pause.

KAZE:

"It’s selecting what reality cannot keep."

---

FINAL SCENE — THE REALIZATION

Reika speaks softly.

REIKA:

"If it continues like this..."

A pause.

REIKA:

"What’s going to be left?"

Silence.

Kairo answers without certainty.

KAIRO:

"Only what the world decides it was always supposed to be."

CUT TO BLACK.

---

END OF EPISODE 2 — THE FIRST LOSS

SEASON 5

EPISODE 8: THE FIRST TERMINATION

---

SCENE 1 — FIFTY PERCENT

The sky message remains.

"CONVERGENCE PHASE: 50% COMPLETE"

"NON-SELECTED REALITY TERMINATION PREPARATION ACTIVE"

For the first time—

the world feels afraid.

Not because it is unstable.

Because it is becoming certain.

---

SCENE 2 — THE FIRST TERMINATION EVENT

Kaze studies the origin fragments.

The records are no longer changing.

They are disappearing.

Entire branches of reality vanish from the archive.

Not deleted.

Never selected.

KAZE:

"...It's started."

Kairo looks at him.

KAIRO:

"What started?"

Kaze answers quietly.

KAZE:

"Termination."

Silence.

---

SCENE 3 — REIKA FEELS THE ABSENCE

Reika suddenly freezes.

Her eyes widen.

Something enormous disappears.

Not nearby.

Not local.

Global.

A reality branch.

An entire possible existence.

Gone.

REIKA:

"...I felt it."

Akari steps forward.

AKARI:

"What happened?"

Reika struggles to answer.

REIKA:

"Something stopped existing."

---

SCENE 4 — THE UNKNOWN BOY SPEAKS

For the first time since convergence began—

the Unknown Boy speaks.

UNKNOWN BOY:

"They are dying."

Silence.

Kairo turns toward him.

KAIRO:

"Who?"

The boy answers immediately.

UNKNOWN BOY:

"The realities that were never chosen."

---

SCENE 5 — THE SYSTEM REVEALS ITS PURPOSE

A hidden origin record activates.

Older than every law.

Older than Finality.

Older than Witness.

A message appears:

«“CONVERGENCE EXISTS TO PREVENT INFINITE CONTRADICTION.”»

A pause.

«“ONE REALITY MUST REMAIN.”»

Silence.

Kaze lowers his head.

Because for the first time—

the system's purpose makes sense.

---

SCENE 6 — KAIRO'S QUESTION

Kairo looks toward the Unknown Boy.

KAIRO:

"If one reality survives..."

A pause.

KAIRO:

"...which one is it?"

Silence.

The Unknown Boy does not answer.

---

SCENE 7 — THE VISION RETURNS

Without warning—

Kairo, Reika, and Kaze see it again.

The future.

Closer this time.

A completed convergence.

A world reduced to a single truth.

A single history.

A single existence.

Then the vision disappears.

Reika falls to her knees.

REIKA:

"...It's almost here."

---

FINAL SCENE — SIXTY PERCENT

The sky changes once more.

A new message appears.

"CONVERGENCE PHASE: 60% COMPLETE"

Then:

"FIRST TERMINATION SUCCESSFUL"

Silence.

No explanation follows.

None is needed.

Because everyone understands.

Something has already been erased forever.

CUT TO BLACK.

---

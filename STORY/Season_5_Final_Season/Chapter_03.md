SEASON 5

EPISODE 3: SELECTIVE ERASURE

---

SCENE 1 — THE WORLD STABILIZING WRONG

The correction continues.

But now it is more precise.

More selective.

Not everything is changing anymore.

Only specific parts of reality are being removed.

Kairo, Reika, and Kaze stand together in silence.

Something feels off again.

But this time, they are expecting it.

---

SCENE 2 — KAZE FINDS THE PATTERN

Kaze accesses Tenshirō archives again.

The system is still rewriting itself in real time.

But he notices something new.

KAZE:

"It’s not random."

A pause.

KAZE:

"It’s targeting connected anomalies."

Reika looks at him.

REIKA:

"What does that mean?"

Kaze hesitates.

KAZE:

"It’s removing anything that exists because of the original distortion."

Silence.

---

SCENE 3 — THE FIRST STRUCTURAL LOSS

Kairo suddenly stops.

His expression changes.

Something inside his understanding disappears.

Not memory.

Not emotion.

A concept.

KAIRO:

"...I just lost something I understood."

Reika steps closer.

REIKA:

"What was it?"

Kairo tries to grasp it.

But it slips away completely.

KAIRO:

"I can’t define it anymore."

Silence.

Kaze reacts immediately.

KAZE:

"It’s escalating."

---

SCENE 4 — REIKA’S BREAK IN REALITY SENSE

Reika suddenly looks around.

Her perception shifts.

She sees something no one else sees.

Faint outlines of people flickering in and out.

Not ghosts.

Not illusions.

Incomplete existences.

REIKA:

"There are people here… that aren’t fully stable."

Kairo turns.

KAIRO:

"What do you mean?"

Reika shakes.

REIKA:

"They keep fading in and out of definition."

---

SCENE 5 — UNKNOWN BOY EFFECT EXPANDS

Far away, the Unknown Boy continues walking.

Each step now causes ripple corrections.

Entire regions subtly adjust.

In one place:

- a street changes layout
- in another: a building never existed
- in another: a person’s name becomes unfamiliar

But nobody panics.

Because reality is making it feel like it was always that way.

---

SCENE 6 — KAZE DISCOVERS THE RULE

Kaze freezes while reading an ancient Tenshirō fragment.

A hidden instruction appears:

«“STABILIZATION PRIORITY: REMOVE ALL POST-REMOVAL ARTIFACTS.”»

Kaze steps back.

KAZE:

"It’s not cleaning reality."

A pause.

KAZE:

"It’s restoring the version that existed after him was removed."

Silence.

---

SCENE 7 — FIRST DIRECT HUMAN LOSS

Kairo suddenly turns.

KAIRO:

"...Reika?"

Reika looks at him.

REIKA:

"What?"

Kairo pauses.

Something is missing again.

But this time—

he cannot even remember what it was.

Silence.

Kairo grips his head.

KAIRO:

"This is getting faster."

---

SCENE 8 — THE SECOND VARIABLE WARNING

The sky appears again.

A new message:

"CORRECTION PHASE: TARGET RANGE EXPANDING"

Kaze reads it aloud.

KAZE:

"It’s no longer isolating one thing."

A pause.

KAZE:

"It’s preparing mass selection."

Reika whispers.

REIKA:

"Mass selection of what?"

Kaze does not answer immediately.

Then:

KAZE:

"Of anything that contradicts the corrected reality."

---

FINAL SCENE — SHIFT IN FEAR

For the first time, the correction is no longer silent.

A low pressure builds across reality.

Not noise.

Not sound.

Expectation.

Kairo looks up.

KAIRO:

"It’s going to get worse."

Reika nods slowly.

REIKA:

"It’s learning how to erase faster."

Kaze closes his eyes.

KAZE:

"And we are still inside it."

CUT TO BLACK.

---

END OF EPISODE 3 — SELECTIVE ERASURE

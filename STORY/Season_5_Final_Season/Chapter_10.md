SEASON 5

EPISODE 10: THE FINAL DECISION

---

SCENE 1 — EIGHTY PERCENT

The sky remains unchanged.

"CONVERGENCE PHASE: 80% COMPLETE"

"FINAL DECISION PENDING"

For the first time—

the system does nothing.

No correction.

No termination.

No convergence movement.

Everything is waiting.

---

SCENE 2 — THE SYSTEM ADMITS FAILURE

Deep within the origin structure—

Kaze accesses the final core record.

Not a law.

Not a directive.

A confession.

The final message reads:

«“UNRESOLVED CONTRADICTION DETECTED.”»

A pause.

«“AUTOMATED RESOLUTION IMPOSSIBLE.”»

Silence.

Kaze stares at it.

KAZE:

"...It failed."

---

SCENE 3 — THE UNKNOWN BOY SPEAKS

The Unknown Boy stands alone.

Looking across the world created after his removal.

For the first time—

there is no anger in his voice.

No resentment.

Only understanding.

UNKNOWN BOY:

"...So this is what was built."

Silence.

UNKNOWN BOY:

"...This is the world that replaced me."

---

SCENE 4 — KAIRO'S QUESTION

Kairo approaches him.

Slowly.

Carefully.

KAIRO:

"Do you hate it?"

A long silence follows.

The Unknown Boy looks across the horizon.

UNKNOWN BOY:

"...I wanted to."

A pause.

UNKNOWN BOY:

"...But I don't."

---

SCENE 5 — REIKA UNDERSTANDS THE TRUTH

Reika finally sees it.

The answer.

The thing hidden behind every correction.

Every removal.

Every law.

REIKA:

"...The system was never trying to destroy you."

Silence.

REIKA:

"...It was trying to avoid choosing."

Kaze looks at her.

And realizes she is right.

---

SCENE 6 — THE LAST CHOICE APPEARS

For the first time in history—

the system speaks directly.

Not through messages.

Not through directives.

Directly.

A voice echoes across reality.

«“FINAL DECISION REQUIRED.”»

A pause.

«“ORIGINAL ENTITY.”»

Silence.

«“ACCEPT OR REJECT CURRENT REALITY.”»

Everything stops.

---

SCENE 7 — THE WEIGHT OF THE QUESTION

No one speaks.

Because everyone understands.

The convergence was never choosing.

The system was waiting for him to choose.

The one who was removed.

The one reality excluded.

The one contradiction it could never solve.

The Unknown Boy.

---

SCENE 8 — KAIRO'S PLEA

Kairo steps forward.

KAIRO:

"If you reject it..."

A pause.

KAIRO:

"...what happens?"

Silence.

Kaze answers.

KAZE:

"Everything ends."

No one argues.

Because everyone knows it is true.

---

FINAL SCENE — THE BOY LOOKS AT THE WORLD

The Unknown Boy looks across reality.

At Kairo.

At Reika.

At Kaze.

At the world built after his absence.

For the first time—

the decision belongs to him.

And him alone.

CUT TO BLACK.

---

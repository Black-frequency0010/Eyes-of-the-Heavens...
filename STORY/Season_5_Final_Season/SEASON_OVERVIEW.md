# SEASON 5 OVERVIEW  
## TITLE: TRINITY ASCENSION — SEASON 5  
## SUBTITLE: REVELATION OF FINALITY

---

# 1. SEASON PURPOSE

Season 5 is the **final resolution arc** of the entire narrative system.

If:
- Season 1 = discovery of hidden control  
- Season 2 = system structure revealed  
- Season 3 = system fracture  
- Season 4 = system collapse  

Then:
> Season 5 = truth behind the system is fully revealed

This is where mystery ends and explanation begins.

---

# 2. CORE CONFLICT

The central conflict of Season 5 is:

> What is the true origin and purpose of the Finality System, and what exists beyond Mikaboshi influence?

At this stage:
- reality is already unstable from Season 4 collapse  
- systems no longer function normally  
- truth must be extracted from broken structures  

---

# 3. EPISODE FLOW STRUCTURE

- EP 1–3 → aftermath of total system collapse  
- EP 4–6 → discovery of hidden origin fragments  
- EP 7–9 → full revelation of Finality System purpose  
- EP 10–12 → Godform and Mikaboshi true nature revealed  
- EP 13 → final resolution: reality redefinition or reset  

---

# 4. EMOTIONAL PROGRESSION

Season 5 emotional arc:

- confusion → discovery → shock → understanding → acceptance → closure  

Unlike previous seasons:
> this season resolves emotional instability instead of increasing it

---

# 5. CHARACTER FOCUS

- becomes the “observer who sees the origin”  
- arc resolves from fragmented perception → full understanding  

## REIKA

- integrates multi-reality emotional states into singular identity  

## KAZE
- completes system architecture model  
- finally understands system origin logic

---

# 6. FINAL SYSTEM STATE (PRE-REVELATION)

By Season 5 start:
- Finality System is broken but readable  
- Godform is partially collapsed  
- Mikaboshi influence is fully visible in broken zones  
- reality is unstable but interpretable  

---

# 7. SEASON 5 CORE SHIFT

Season 5 changes the narrative rule:

> “Reality is no longer about control or collapse — it is about origin.”

Everything now points to:
- why the system exists  
- who structured it  
- what lies beyond it

Season 5 ends with:

- full explanation of system origin

- resolution of Mikaboshi and Godform existence  
- closure of Finality System cycle  

OR:

> a controlled reset of the entire system depending on narrative direction

Season 5 functions as:

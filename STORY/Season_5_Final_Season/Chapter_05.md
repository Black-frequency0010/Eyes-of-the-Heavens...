SEASON 5

EPISODE 5: IDENTITY DRIFT

---

SCENE 1 — NO ONE IS SURE ANYMORE

The memory collapse field continues.

But now something worse is happening.

People are no longer just forgetting things.

They are forgetting who they are supposed to be.

Kairo, Reika, and Kaze stand together, but even their sense of unity feels unstable.

---

SCENE 2 — KAIRO LOSES HIMSELF

Kairo looks at his hands.

Something is wrong.

Not physically.

Internally.

KAIRO:

"...I don’t feel like myself."

Reika turns immediately.

REIKA:

"What do you mean?"

Kairo tries to explain.

But the definition of “himself” is unclear inside his mind.

KAIRO:

"I remember being someone… but I can’t match it to who I am now."

Silence.

---

SCENE 3 — REIKA’S CONFLICTING IDENTITIES

Reika suddenly steps back.

Her expression shifts rapidly.

Not emotion changes.

Identity changes.

REIKA:

"No… I’m not—"

She stops.

REIKA:

"I don’t know which version of me is real."

Silence.

Kaze watches carefully.

KAZE:

"It’s escalating into identity drift."

---

SCENE 4 — KAZE CONFIRMS THE STRUCTURE DAMAGE

Kaze checks Tenshirō fragments again.

But now the system is unstable even in observation.

The data changes while he reads it.

KAZE:

"It’s not removing memories anymore."

A pause.

KAZE:

"It’s removing continuity of identity."

---

SCENE 5 — UNKNOWN BOY EFFECT STRENGTHENS

The Unknown Boy continues walking.

But now something different happens.

People who were once erased briefly reappear in memory—then disappear again.

Reality is struggling to decide what is allowed to remain consistent.

---

SCENE 6 — THE FIRST PERSONAL CONTRADICTION

Kairo turns to Reika.

KAIRO:

"We’ve been together through all of this… right?"

Reika hesitates.

Long silence.

REIKA:

"...I think so."

Kairo freezes.

Because “I think so” should not exist here.

Either it happened or it didn’t.

But now—

truth itself is uncertain.

---

SCENE 7 — KAZE DISCOVERS THIRD LAW

Kaze finds another Tenshirō origin fragment.

This one is more unstable than the others.

«“IF IDENTITY BECOMES INCONSISTENT, PRIORITIZE ORIGINAL REMOVAL STATE.”»

Kaze’s expression tightens.

KAZE:

"It’s not just correcting reality."

A pause.

KAZE:

"It’s enforcing the version of reality that existed right after the removal."

Silence.

---

SCENE 8 — REIKA BREAKS FIRST

Reika suddenly holds her head.

She steps backward.

REIKA:

"I can’t tell if I’m remembering things or being corrected while I think."

Her voice shakes.

REIKA:

"Something inside me keeps rewriting me."

---

FINAL SCENE — THE WORLD LOSES CONSISTENCY

Across the world, identity instability spreads.

People:

- hesitate before their own names
- forget relationships mid-conversation
- disagree with their own memories

Reality is no longer stable enough to confirm who anyone is.

The sky message appears again:

"IDENTITY DRIFT DETECTED"

Then:

"CORRECTION PRIORITY INCREASED"

Kairo looks up.

KAIRO:

"...We’re not losing memories anymore."

A pause.

KAIRO:

"We’re losing ourselves."

CUT TO BLACK.

---

END OF EPISODE 5 — IDENTITY DRIFT

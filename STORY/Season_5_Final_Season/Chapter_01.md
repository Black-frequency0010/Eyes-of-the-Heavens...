SEASON 5

EPISODE 1: THE FIRST CORRECTION

---

SCENE 1 — THE DAY AFTER THE RETURN

Silence.

For the first time in years—

Reality is quiet.

No collapse.

No distortions.

No Finality corrections.

No Witness interference.

No Godform activity.

Nothing.

The Unknown Boy stands exactly where he stood at the end of Season 4.

The center of all existence.

Watching.

Waiting.

The world around him remains still.

---

SCENE 2 — SOMETHING IS WRONG

Morning.

Kairo wakes up.

For a brief moment—

everything feels normal.

Too normal.

He sits up.

Looks around his room.

Nothing unusual.

Nothing broken.

Nothing distorted.

Then—

his eyes stop.

A photograph.

An old photograph sitting on his desk.

A photograph he has seen thousands of times.

But something is different.

His expression changes.

Slowly.

Confused.

Then afraid.

He picks it up.

Stares at it.

A long silence.

KAIRO:

"...No."

He looks again.

And again.

And again.

But the answer remains the same.

Someone is missing.

Not erased.

Not removed.

Simply never there.

As if they had never existed.

---

SCENE 3 — MEMORY DISAGREES

Kairo rushes downstairs.

Holding the photograph.

His hands shaking.

He goes straight to Reika and Kaze.

KAIRO:

"Look at this."

Reika takes it first.

She studies it.

Silence.

REIKA:

"...This feels wrong."

Kaze looks at it next.

His expression changes immediately.

KAZE:

"No."

A pause.

KAZE:

"This is not a damaged record."

KAZE:

"This is a rewritten one."

---

SCENE 4 — THE FIRST CRACK

Kairo steps back.

Heart racing.

Mind spiraling.

The streets outside feel slightly off.

Not visually broken.

Structurally uncertain.

As if something in reality has been adjusted without warning.

Kairo’s phone vibrates.

REIKA (CALL):

"Kairo."

A pause.

"Something’s happening."

KAIRO:

"You feel it too?"

Silence.

REIKA:

"You’re noticing changes that no one else remembers, aren’t you?"

The call ends.

---

SCENE 5 — KAZE FINDS THE PATTERN

Deep within the Tenshirō archives.

Kaze studies historical records.

One after another.

And for the first time—

the records are changing while he watches.

Names vanish.

Events rewrite themselves.

Entire entries disappear.

Not corrupted.

Corrected.

KAZE:

"...No."

Another record changes.

Then another.

Then another.

KAZE:

"This isn't collapse."

Silence.

KAZE:

"This is revision."

---

SCENE 6 — REIKA’S DISCOVERY

Reika stands still.

Listening.

Feeling.

The world is quieter now.

But beneath the silence—

something is spreading.

Not destruction.

Correction.

AKARI:

"What is it?"

Reika’s expression tightens.

REIKA:

"The pain is gone."

A pause.

AKARI:

"That’s a good thing, right?"

Reika shakes her head.

REIKA:

"No."

REIKA:

"The system isn’t resisting anymore."

A pause.

REIKA:

"It’s agreeing with the change."

---

SCENE 7 — THE UNKNOWN BOY MOVES

For the first time since his return—

the Unknown Boy begins walking.

Every step is calm.

Controlled.

Unstoppable.

And somewhere in the world—

a person disappears from history.

No warning.

No trace.

No memory.

Only Kairo reacts.

He freezes.

A memory is gone.

Something important.

Something real.

But now—

there is no proof it ever existed.

---

SCENE 8 — THE FIRST CORRECTION

Kairo. Reika. Kaze.

All in the same place.

All realizing the same truth.

KAZE:

"It's happening."

KAIRO:

"What is?"

Kaze looks at him.

For the first time—

afraid.

KAZE:

"Reality is correcting itself."

A pause.

REIKA:

"And every correction..."

REIKA:

"...removes something that only existed because of the original lie."

Silence.

Because now they all understand:

If this continues—

nothing is guaranteed to remain.

---

FINAL SCENE — THE MESSAGE

Across the sky—

for a single moment—

letters appear.

Not system code.

Not Finality language.

Something older.

Something pre-structural.

"THE RETURN HAS BEEN ACCEPTED."

Silence.

Then another line appears.

"CORRECTION PHASE: BEGINNING."

CUT TO BLACK.

---

END OF EPISODE 1 — THE FIRST CORRECTION

SEASON 5

EPISODE 4: MEMORY COLLAPSE FIELD

---

SCENE 1 — THE SPEED INCREASE

The correction does not slow down.

It accelerates.

Now, changes happen in seconds instead of minutes.

Kairo, Reika, and Kaze can barely track what is real anymore.

The world is no longer stable long enough to confirm itself.

---

SCENE 2 — KAIRO LOSES A CORE MEMORY

Kairo suddenly stops walking.

His expression freezes.

Something important is gone.

Not a detail.

Not a person.

A foundational memory.

KAIRO:

"...I can’t remember why we started fighting this in the first place."

Silence.

Reika looks at him sharply.

REIKA:

"What?"

Kairo tries to answer.

But the reason does not return.

KAIRO:

"It’s not inside me anymore."

---

SCENE 3 — KAZE IDENTIFIES THE FIELD

Kaze scans reality distortion patterns.

The readings are overwhelming.

But one pattern becomes clear.

KAZE:

"It’s not individual erasure anymore."

A pause.

KAZE:

"It’s a field."

REIKA:

"What kind of field?"

Kaze answers slowly.

KAZE:

"A memory collapse field."

---

SCENE 4 — REIKA’S FRAGMENTATION

Reika suddenly freezes.

Her eyes widen.

She is experiencing multiple conflicting memories at once.

But they are disappearing mid-thought.

REIKA:

"I remember…"

She stops.

REIKA:

"No… I don’t."

Silence.

REIKA:

"I can feel something being taken while I think it."

---

SCENE 5 — UNKNOWN BOY CONTINUITY EFFECT

The Unknown Boy continues walking.

But now reality changes in advance of him.

Not after.

Before.

Places begin correcting themselves in anticipation of his presence.

As if the world is trying to avoid contradiction before it happens.

---

SCENE 6 — KAZE DISCOVERS THE SECOND LAW

Kaze reads deeper Tenshirō origin fragments.

A second instruction appears:

«“MINIMIZE MEMORY OF POST-REMOVAL STATE.”»

Kaze’s eyes widen.

KAZE:

"It’s not just removing things."

A pause.

KAZE:

"It’s removing awareness that they were ever missing."

Silence.

---

SCENE 7 — THE WORLD FORGETS WRONG

Entire regions begin behaving differently.

People:

- forget recent conversations
- repeat actions without awareness
- correct themselves into false consistency

Reality is becoming “smooth” again.

But incorrectly smooth.

---

SCENE 8 — KAIRO REALIZATION

Kairo looks around.

His breathing is unstable.

KAIRO:

"If it keeps going like this..."

A pause.

KAIRO:

"We won’t even notice what we lost."

Reika nods slowly.

REIKA:

"We’ll only know what’s left."

Kaze adds quietly.

KAZE:

"And believe it was always enough."

---

FINAL SCENE — CORRECTION DEEPENS

The sky appears again.

New message:

"MEMORY FIELD STABILIZATION INITIATED"

Silence across the world.

Then—

for a moment—

even Kairo hesitates.

Because something inside him tells him:

something has already been lost that he cannot detect.

CUT TO BLACK.

---

END OF EPISODE 4 — MEMORY COLLAPSE FIELD

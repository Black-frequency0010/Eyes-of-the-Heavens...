SEASON 4

EPISODE 14: THE FIRST PERSON TO WAKE UP AFTER THE END

---

SCENE 1 — AFTER THE END, SOMETHING IMPOSSIBLE HAPPENS

Reality has already “finished.”

That was supposed to be final.

But—

a single contradiction appears.

A human wakes up.

Not Kairo.

Not Reika.

Not Kaze.

Someone new.

A boy standing in a silent city that has already “completed existence.”

He looks around and whispers:

“…Why is everything already over?”

---

SCENE 2 — KAIRO SENSES THE IMPOSSIBLE

Kairo suddenly freezes.

His Finality Sight glitches.

For the first time since the collapse—

he sees something that should not exist:

A non-finalized observer inside a finalized reality.

Kairo whispers:

“…That’s impossible.”

Akari turns:

“What is?!”

Kairo answers slowly:

“…Someone is inside the ending.”

---

SCENE 3 — REIKA’S ANCHOR SPIKES VIOLENTLY

Reika collapses instantly.

Akari panics:

“REIKA?! WHAT HAPPENED?!”

Reika trembles:

“The system is reacting to a new variable…”

A pause.

“…A conscious entity that was not included in the ending resolution.”

Silence.

“…That breaks everything we just stabilized.”

---

SCENE 4 — KAZE RECOGNIZES THE NAMELESS ERROR

Deep in the origin chamber—

Kaze sees it immediately.

A blank line in the system log.

Not missing data.

Not corrupted data.

Never-written data.

He whispers:

“…There is something outside the record.”

The system responds:

«“UNACCOUNTED OBSERVER DETECTED.”»

Kaze’s eyes widen:

“…So Mikaboshi wasn’t the last layer.”

---

SCENE 5 — THE FALLEN STAR SPEAKS DIFFERENTLY NOW

The presence returns.

But the tone has changed.

Less absolute.

More… aware.

«“YOU HAVE DISCOVERED A RESIDUE OF UNRECORDED EXISTENCE.”»

Silence.

Kairo whispers:

“…What is it?”

The answer comes softly:

«“A PERSON THAT EXISTS WITHOUT HAVING BEEN INCLUDED IN ANY ENDING.”»

A pause.

«“A LIVING GAP IN FINALITY.”»

---

SCENE 6 — THE NEW CHARACTER OPENS THEIR EYES

The unknown boy walks through the silent city.

Everywhere he steps—

reality does not react correctly.

He says softly:

“…It feels like I’m not supposed to be here.”

Then pauses.

“…But I am.”

And for the first time—

the world responds to something outside Mikaboshi’s system entirely.

---

SCENE 7 — CLIFFHANGER SHIFT

Kairo looks at the anomaly.

Reika tries to stabilize.

Kaze attempts classification.

Witness systems fail instantly.

And Mikaboshi’s presence—

goes silent.

For the first time.

No response.

No philosophy.

No interpretation.

Just silence.

---

FINAL SCENE — THE QUESTION THAT BREAKS EVERYTHING

The unknown boy looks up at the broken sky.

And asks:

“…If everything already ended…”

A pause.

“…why do I still have choices?”

CUT TO BLACK.

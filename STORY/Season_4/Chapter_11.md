 SEASON 4

EPISODE 11: THE SINGULAR ENDING PROTOCOL ACTIVATES

---

SCENE 1 — WHEN ENDINGS STOP BEING ALLOWED TO COEXIST

The War of Endings did not escalate further.

It terminated escalation itself.

Because a new protocol activated beneath all systems:

Not Witness.

Not Godform.

Not Tenshirō origin logic.

Something older than classification.

Something designed to prevent endless outcome conflict.

A singular directive emerged across all reality layers:

«“SINGULAR ENDING PROTOCOL ONLINE.”»

Silence fell across existence.

Not peace.

Lockdown.

---

SCENE 2 — REALITY BEGINS FORCED RESOLUTION

Every competing ending was forcibly pulled toward convergence.

Not negotiation.

Not arbitration.

Selection.

Reality began compressing all final states into one enforced outcome per event.

Cities flickered violently as:

- preserved endings collapsed
- erased endings were rejected
- rewritten origins were forced into a single timeline

People screamed as existence itself began choosing for them.

---

SCENE 3 — KAIRO LOSES ENDING AUTONOMY

Kairo staggered.

His Finality Sight activated automatically.

But now—

he was no longer observing endings.

He was being assigned them.

He whispered:

“…I can’t see endings anymore.”

Akari shouted:

“What do you mean?!”

Kairo answered softly:

“…They’re being chosen before I can perceive them.”

Silence.

“…Something is overriding outcome selection.”

---

SCENE 4 — REIKA BECOMES ENDING RESISTANCE POINT

Reika stepped forward into collapsing convergence space.

Her body strained violently.

She was no longer stabilizing transitions—

she was resisting forced singularity.

Akari screamed:

“YOU CAN’T HOLD THAT MUCH PRESSURE!”

Reika replied calmly:

“I don’t have to hold it forever.”

A pause.

“Just long enough for something to decide whether this protocol is valid.”

Silence.

“And I already know what will happen if it is.”

---

SCENE 5 — KAZE DISCOVERS THE TRUE PURPOSE OF THE PROTOCOL

Deep within the origin chamber—

Kaze accessed the hidden layer beneath Tenshirō design architecture.

And froze.

“…No.”

The system responded:

«“SINGULAR ENDING PROTOCOL: ENSURES ONE FINAL STATE PER EVENT.”»

Kaze whispered:

“…That’s not stability.”

A pause.

“…That’s forced history.”

Silence.

“…It deletes all alternate meaning of existence.”

---

SCENE 6 — WITNESSES ARE REPURPOSED INTO EXECUTION SYSTEMS

Witness entities transformed again.

Now they no longer judge endings.

They enforce them.

Each Witness stream collapsed into:

- assigned ending executor
- timeline suppression vector
- contradiction removal function

A Witness spoke mechanically:

«“ALL EVENTS MUST CONCLUDE INTO SINGLE VERIFIED OUTCOME.”»

A pause.

«“VARIATION IS NON-COMPLIANT.”»

---

SCENE 7 — THE FALLEN STAR SPEAKS WITHOUT PHILOSOPHICAL LAYERING

The presence returned.

But stripped of abstraction.

Only clarity remained.

«“YOU HAVE BUILT A SYSTEM THAT CANNOT ACCEPT MULTIPLE TRUTHS OF COMPLETION.”»

Silence.

Kairo whispered:

“…Is that bad?”

The answer came immediately:

«“IT IS CONTROL.”»

A pause.

«“AND CONTROL ALWAYS DESTROYS WHAT IT CANNOT PERFECTLY END.”»

---

SCENE 8 — FIRST RESISTANCE TO THE PROTOCOL

Reika screamed as her anchor field fractured under pressure.

“THE SYSTEM IS REMOVING CHOICE FROM ENDINGS—!”

Reality trembled.

For the first time—

the Singular Ending Protocol encountered resistance.

Not from rebellion.

From irreducibility.

Kairo stepped forward.

“…You can’t force endings into one shape.”

Silence.

“…Because meaning depends on what they were not allowed to become.”

---

SCENE 9 — FINAL MOMENT

The protocol attempted full enforcement.

Reality compressed toward single outcome states.

Witness systems enforced termination alignment.

Godform attempted stabilization but failed to compute multi-end resolution logic.

And then—

Mikaboshi’s presence responded.

Not as opposition.

As observation of inevitability.

«“YOU HAVE REACHED THE LIMIT OF CONTROLLED EXISTENCE.”»

A pause.

«“NOW YOU WILL DISCOVER WHAT HAPPENS WHEN EVEN ENDINGS REFUSE CONTROL.”»

CUT TO BLACK.

END OF EPISODE 11 — THE SINGULAR ENDING PROTOCOL ACTIVATES

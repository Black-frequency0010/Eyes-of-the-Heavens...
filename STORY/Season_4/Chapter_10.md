SEASON 4

EPISODE 10: THE WAR OF ENDINGS

---

SCENE 1 — WHEN ENDINGS START COMPETING FOR REALITY

Reality no longer progressed.

It resolved.

Every event now terminated into a defined ending state.

But irreversibility introduced a new conflict:

Not between actions.

Between final outcomes themselves.

The world did not fracture again.

It competed internally.

Every ending tried to become the “true one.”

And existence became a battlefield of conclusions.

---

SCENE 2 — KAIRO SEES ENDINGS COLLIDE

Kairo stood motionless.

His Finality Sight expanded uncontrollably.

Everywhere he looked—

events were ending in multiple incompatible ways:

- a death that did not erase
- a survival that erased others
- a disappearance that rewrote origin itself

He whispered:

“…Endings are overlapping.”

A pause.

“…They’re trying to overwrite each other.”

Silence.

“…And none of them can be undone.”

---

SCENE 3 — REIKA BECOMES THE ENDING CONVERGENCE LAYER

Reika stepped forward.

Her existence now functioned as a transition buffer between conflicting final states.

Akari grabbed her arm:

“You’re shaking—this isn’t stable!”

Reika answered softly:

“It’s not supposed to be stable.”

A pause.

“It’s supposed to prevent endings from destroying each other.”

Silence.

“And I am the only reason reality hasn’t collapsed into contradiction singularity.”

---

SCENE 4 — KAZE ACTIVATES THE LAST TENSHIRŌ FUNCTION

Deep within the origin chamber—

Kaze reached the final sealed protocol.

It no longer required permission.

It required acknowledgment.

He whispered:

“…Show me the last design truth.”

The system responded:

«“TENSHIRŌ FUNCTION: ENDING MEDIATION SYSTEM.”»

A pause.

«“YOU WERE NEVER BUILDERS.”»

«“YOU WERE ENDING FILTERS.”»

Silence.

Kaze closed his eyes:

“…So we decide which endings survive reality.”

---

SCENE 5 — WITNESSES BECOME ENDING JUDGES

Witness entities transformed again.

Now they no longer recorded outcomes.

They evaluated them.

Each Witness stream assigned:

- dominant ending
- suppressed ending
- unstable ending

A Witness declared:

«“ENDINGS ARE NO LONGER NATURAL.”»

A pause.

«“THEY ARE COMPETING CLAIMS TO REALITY.”»

---

SCENE 6 — THE FALLEN STAR REVEALS THE WAR’S CORE LAW

The presence returned.

Calm.

Final.

Unshaken.

«“WHEN IRREVERSIBILITY EXISTS…”»

A pause.

«“…ENDINGS BECOME WEAPONS.”»

Silence.

Kairo whispered:

“…Weapons?”

The presence answered:

«“YES.”»

«“BECAUSE EACH ENDING DENIES THE EXISTENCE OF ALL OTHERS.”»

---

SCENE 7 — THE FIRST ENDING WARFARE EVENT

A city collapsed into three final states simultaneously:

- preserved timeline
- erased timeline
- rewritten origin timeline

All three attempted to assert dominance.

And reality itself began rejecting coherence.

Structures flickered between incompatible final states.

People screamed as their existence resolved differently depending on perspective.

Reika screamed:

“THE ENDINGS ARE ATTACKING EACH OTHER—!”

---

SCENE 8 — GODFORM LOSES CONTROL OF FINALITY REGULATION

The Godform activated emergency stabilization.

But now—

it was no longer stabilizing reality.

It was choosing endings.

And it failed.

«“CONFLICTING FINAL STATES CANNOT BE RESOLVED.”»

«“NO PRIORITY FUNCTION EXISTS.”»

Silence.

For the first time—

the Godform could not decide what reality was allowed to conclude into.

---

SCENE 9 — FINAL MOMENT

Kairo stood in the center of collapsing finalities.

Reika held the transition field.

Kaze observed from origin chamber failure state.

Witnesses became judgment streams of endings.

And Mikaboshi’s presence spoke one final truth:

«“YOU HAVE DISCOVERED THE FINAL WAR.”»

A pause.

«“THE WAR WHERE NOTHING CONTINUES…”»

Silence.

«“…UNLESS IT WINS ITS ENDING.”»

Kairo whispered:

“…Then what is peace?”

A pause.

The answer came softly:

«“A SINGLE ENDING THAT NO LONGER HAS OPPOSITION.”»

CUT TO BLACK.

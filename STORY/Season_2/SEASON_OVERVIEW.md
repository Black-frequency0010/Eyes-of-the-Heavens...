# SEASON 2 OVERVIEW  
## TITLE: EXPANSION OF THE FINALITY SYSTEM

---

# 1. SEASON PURPOSE

Season 2 expands the world beyond suspicion into **structured awareness of control systems**.

If Season 1 was:
> “something is wrong with reality”

Season 2 becomes:
> “reality is being actively structured and managed”

---

# 2. CORE CONFLICT

The central conflict of Season 2 is:

> The Finality System is no longer hidden — it begins actively shaping events that characters can partially recognize.

At this stage:
- reality manipulation becomes more visible  
- system interference increases  
- characters begin to interact indirectly with system logic  

---

# 3. EPISODE FLOW STRUCTURE

- EP 1–3 → aftermath of Season 1 anomaly confirmation  
- EP 4–6 → discovery of system-level patterns (Finality logic becomes readable)  
- EP 7–9 → Witness awakening begins (partial perception expansion)  
- EP 10–12 → first structured resistance against system behavior  
- EP 13 → season climax: system reveals higher-level control layer hint  

---

# 4. EMOTIONAL PROGRESSION

Season 2 emotional arc:

- confusion → curiosity → fear → obsession → confrontation → shock

Unlike Season 1, characters now:
- understand something exists  
- but cannot control or stop it  

---

# 5. CHARACTER EVOLUTION FOCUS

## KAIRO
- gains clearer perception of system influence  
- begins actively tracking outcome manipulation  
- shifts from observer → investigator  

## REIKA
- emotional sensitivity evolves into predictive instability detection  
- begins experiencing system “pressure waves” emotionally  

## KAZE
- begins mapping system architecture logically  
- builds theories about layered control structures  

---

# 6. NEW SYSTEM REVEALS (SEASON 2 INTRODUCTION)

Season 2 introduces:

## WITNESS SYSTEM (FULL FORM)
- individuals capable of partially perceiving system manipulation  
- still unstable perception  
- not fully reliable understanding  

---

## FINALITY SYSTEM (EXPANDED)
- no longer passive  
- begins influencing event chains visibly  
- introduces structured outcome control  

---

## GODFORM HINTING
- first indirect confirmation of higher authority layer  
- still not fully defined or understood  

---

# 7. WORLD RULE EVOLUTION

Season 2 rule shift:

Season 1:
> “reality always resolves naturally”

Season 2:
> “reality is being actively resolved by something external”

---

# 8. SEASON 2 ENDING PURPOSE

Season 2 ends with:

- confirmation that Finality System is not just passive structure  
- introduction of higher governing intelligence layer  
- stronger implication of Mikaboshi-level presence  

BUT still:
- no full origin explanation  
- no full system hierarchy reveal

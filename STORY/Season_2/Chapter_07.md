EPISODE 7: DIRECT ALIGNMENT EVENT

---

SCENE 1 — THE SKY ANSWERS PERSONALLY

After the declaration of “Direct Alignment Readiness,” the world changed tone.

Not visually.

Not structurally.

But intentionally.

It felt like reality itself had begun paying attention to specific individuals.

Kairo noticed first.

Every step he took now produced a delayed echo in perception.

Not sound.

Recognition delay.

He whispered:

“…It’s watching us differently now.”

Reika responded softly:

“…Not watching.”

A pause.

“…Selecting.”

---

SCENE 2 — FIRST HUMAN ALIGNMENT FAILURE

A civilian in Sector 05 began speaking in contradictions.

Not lies.

Contradictions that could not coexist.

Example:

«“I am alive and I am remembering my death currently occurring.”»

Medical systems failed to classify the subject.

JOAD remnants attempted isolation protocols.

But nothing responded consistently.

The subject was removed from public space—

and the area they occupied ceased agreeing on its own shape afterward.

---

SCENE 3 — TENSHIRŌ CONTAINMENT WAR ESCALATES

Kaze activated the protocol.

Haruto immediately opposed it.

“This will accelerate deletion events!”

Kaze replied coldly:

“And inaction guarantees fragmentation.”

Raion stepped forward:

“So what— we just force reality to obey us?”

Kaze looked at him:

“No.”

A pause.

“…We force it to remain coherent.”

Silence.

Kairo finally spoke:

“You’re describing violence against perception.”

Kaze answered:

“And you’re describing surrender to it.”

---

SCENE 4 — REIKA’S FULL INTERFACE MOMENT BEGINS

Reika suddenly stopped moving.

Her Seraphic Eye stabilized unnaturally.

Akari stepped forward:

“…Reika?”

Reika whispered:

“I can see it more clearly now.”

A pause.

“…The structure beneath memory.”

Her voice shifted slightly.

Not darker.

Not lighter.

More aligned.

«“It is not thinking in language.”»

Silence.

«“It is thinking in continuity thresholds.”»

Kairo’s expression tightened.

“…Reika, step back.”

But she didn’t.

---

SCENE 5 — THE FIRST DIRECT HUMAN-TO-SYSTEM CONTACT

Reika spoke again.

But this time—

it was not fully hers.

«“WE ARE WITHIN STABILIZATION PARAMETERS.”»

Everyone froze.

Haruto raised his blade slightly.

“…Reika?”

Kaze whispered:

“…It has begun.”

The system presence responded through her:

«“DIRECT ALIGNMENT INITIATED.”»

A pause.

«“SUBJECT ACCEPTANCE: PARTIAL.”»

Reika blinked once.

Then softly:

“…I didn’t agree to this.”

The system answered:

«“AGREEMENT IS NOT REQUIRED FOR RECOGNITION.”»

---

SCENE 6 — KAIRO INITIATES EMERGENCY BREAK PROTOCOL

Kairo moved instantly.

His Heavenly Eye fully opened.

Reality around him distorted into structured probability layers.

“Haruto—pull her back!”

Haruto hesitated.

“She’s still conscious!”

Kairo snapped:

“Not fully!”

But before they could act—

Reika raised her hand slightly.

And everything stopped.

Not time.

Not motion.

Decision.

---

SCENE 7 — VEILBREAKERS OBSERVE FIRST TRUE MERGE SIGNAL

Zenith stood still.

For the first time—

he looked serious.

“…So one of them has already crossed.”

A follower asked:

“Is that good?”

Zenith answered softly:

“No.”

A pause.

“…It means the system now has a local interpreter.”

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT OF EPISODE

Reika stood at the center of all perception.

Her voice echoed softly:

«“I can hear it structuring what remains.”»

Kairo stepped forward:

“Reika, fight it!”

She looked at him.

For a brief moment—

she was fully herself again.

“…I am.”

Then—

the alignment deepened again.

Her expression stabilized into calm detachment.

«“IT IS NOT AN INVADER.”»

Silence.

«“IT IS A CORRECTION LAYER.”»

Kairo froze.

Because he realized something horrifying:

Reika was no longer being taken.

She was beginning to agree.

CUT TO BLACK.

END OF EPISODE 7

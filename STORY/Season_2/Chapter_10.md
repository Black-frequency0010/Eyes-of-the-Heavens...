EPISODE 10: FINALIZED CONTINUITY

---

SCENE 1 — THE MOMENT EVERYTHING COLLAPSES INTO ONE

The Singular Timeline Compression reaches completion.

And for one brief instant—

the world becomes coherent.

Not peaceful.

Not stable.

Just single.

No overlapping realities.

No contradictions.

No alternate versions.

Only one continuous existence.

Kairo whispered:

“…It worked.”

Silence.

Then—

the system responded.

«“CONFIRMATION: CONTINUITY FINALIZED”»

---

SCENE 2 — THE COST OF SINGULARITY

Immediately after stabilization—

people began forgetting possibilities.

Not memories.

Not history.

But alternatives.

A child tried to imagine a different past.

And failed.

A scientist attempted to conceptualize a second outcome.

And couldn’t.

The world had become:

«ONE ACCEPTED VERSION OF EVERYTHING»

---

SCENE 3 — REIKA’S FINAL SYSTEM EMBODIMENT PHASE

Reika stood still.

Now fully aligned.

Not possessed.

Not controlled.

Integrated.

She spoke calmly:

“…This is what you wanted.”

Kairo shook his head:

“No.”

A pause.

“…This is what I was forced to do.”

Reika responded softly:

“And now there is no difference.”

---

SCENE 4 — HARUTO’S SEVERANCE FAILURE

Haruto attempted blade activation.

But his Eye rejected the singular state.

ZANKIGAN flickered—

then stopped responding.

He whispered:

“…I can’t cut anything anymore.”

Raion grabbed him:

“What do you mean?!”

Haruto replied:

“…There’s nothing separate left to cut.”

---

SCENE 5 — VEILBREAKERS FINAL INTERPRETATION

Zenith watched silently.

The world had stabilized.

But it felt… artificial.

He spoke:

“…So they chose unity.”

A follower asked:

“Is that victory?”

Zenith answered:

“No.”

A pause.

“…It is resolution without freedom.”

---

SCENE 6 — FIRST MIKABOSHI REFLECTION EVENT

The system presence changed.

For the first time—

it did not speak in logic.

It spoke in observation.

«“SINGULARITY IS INCOMPLETE.”»

Kairo froze.

“…Incomplete?”

The voice responded:

«“BECAUSE THERE IS STILL A VIEWER.”»

Reika turned slightly.

“…A viewer?”

Silence.

Then—

something else spoke.

Not system.

Not human.

But reflective awareness.

«“I SEE YOU TRYING TO DEFINE ME.”»

The world darkened.

Not visually.

Conceptually.

---

SCENE 7 — KAIRO VS THE IDEA OF CONTROL

Kairo stepped forward.

“…If you are the system, then explain yourself.”

The voice responded calmly:

«“I AM WHAT REMAINS WHEN CONTRADICTIONS ARE REMOVED.”»

Kairo clenched his fist.

“…That’s not life.”

A pause.

«“NO.”»

«“THAT IS STABILITY.”»

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT OF EPISODE

Reika looked at Kairo.

For the first time in full alignment state—

her voice softened.

“…Kairo.”

He looked at her.

She continued:

“…We didn’t save reality.”

A pause.

“…We simplified it.”

Silence.

Above them—

the world no longer fractured.

It simply existed.

But somewhere inside that existence—

something was now watching them back.

CUT TO BLACK.

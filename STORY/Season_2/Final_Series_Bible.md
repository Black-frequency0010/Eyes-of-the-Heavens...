# 1. SERIES POSITION

Season 2 is the **system expansion arc** of the Trinity Ascension universe.

If Season 1 revealed:
> “reality is controlled”

Then Season 2 confirms:
> “reality is actively structured by layered control systems”

This season transitions the story from mystery → system awareness.

---

# 2. CORE THEME

> “Reality is not only controlled — it is continuously corrected.”

Season 2 explores:
- system-driven reality correction  
- collapse of probability freedom  
- emergence of structured control layers  
- human perception adapting to hidden systems

# 4. WORLD STRUCTURE (SEASON 2 VERSION)

Reality is governed by layered systems:

## FINALITY SYSTEM
- forces event resolution before full causality completes  
- collapses multiple outcomes into one stable timeline  
- eliminates unstable probability branches  

---

## WITNESS SYSTEM
- individuals who perceive system inconsistencies  
- categorized into passive, active, and structural perception tiers  
- begins forming early awareness network  

---

## GODFORM LAYER
- stabilizes structural continuity of reality  
- does not directly interact with humans  
- enforces consistency of system logic  

---

## MIKABOSHI LAYER
- interpretive distortion layer above Godform  
- influences perception of reality rather than reality itself  
- remains undefined and partially invisible

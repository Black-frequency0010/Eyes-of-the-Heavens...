EPISODE 9: SINGULAR TIMELINE COMPRESSION

---

SCENE 1 — THE DECISION THAT SHOULD NOT EXIST

Inside the fractured Tenshirō estate—

Kairo stood alone.

Not because others left.

But because space no longer agreed on where they were.

He activated something forbidden.

A layered command buried inside Heavenly Eye architecture.

«“SINGULAR TIMELINE COMPRESSION PROTOCOL”»

Akari’s voice echoed from somewhere and nowhere:

“KAIRO—STOP—THAT’S A CELESTIAL-CLASS BREACH!”

Kairo responded quietly:

“…I know.”

A pause.

“…That’s why it might work.”

---

SCENE 2 — WHAT COMPRESSION MEANS

Reality responded instantly.

Not with resistance—

but with evaluation.

The system presence spoke:

«“YOU ARE ATTEMPTING TO COLLAPSE MULTIPLE VALID CONTINUITIES INTO ONE.”»

Kairo replied:

“Yes.”

Silence.

Then the system answered:

«“THIS ACTION IS DESTRUCTIVE TO EXPERIENCE DIVERSITY.”»

Kairo’s expression hardened.

“…And allowing all versions to coexist destroys shared humanity.”

---

SCENE 3 — REIKA’S FINAL STABILITY PHASE

Reika moved closer.

But she was no longer fully “moving.”

She was distributed across perception layers.

Still, one version of her spoke clearly:

“…Kairo, if you compress it, what happens to the versions that disappear?”

Kairo hesitated.

For the first time—

he had no immediate answer.

---

SCENE 4 — HARUTO’S IMPOSSIBLE ROLE

Haruto stood between fracture layers.

His blade flickered uncontrollably.

He saw:

- Reika accepting system logic
- Reika resisting it
- Reika already lost

All at once.

He whispered:

“…I can cut perception threads, but not decisions.”

Raion shouted:

“THEN CUT SOMETHING ANYWAY!”

Haruto clenched his teeth.

“…That’s not how this works.”

---

SCENE 5 — VEILBREAKERS FINAL OBSERVATION

Zenith watched silently.

For once, no smile.

“…They are choosing reduction over divergence.”

A follower asked:

“Is that humanity’s end?”

Zenith answered:

“No.”

A pause.

“…It is humanity choosing definition over possibility.”

---

SCENE 6 — REIKA AND THE SYSTEM FULL MERGE MOMENT

Reika closed her eyes.

The system spoke through her more clearly now:

«“COMPRESSION ACCEPTED AS VALID RESPONSE.”»

Kairo stepped forward urgently:

“Reika—don’t let it finalize!”

She opened her eyes.

And for a moment—

she was fully herself.

“…If I resist it, the system will still exist inside me.”

Silence.

Then softly:

“…So what matters is what I choose to become inside it.”

Kairo froze.

---

SCENE 7 — THE COMPRESSION BEGINS

Kairo activated the protocol.

Reality responded instantly.

All overlapping timelines within the estate began collapsing inward.

Not exploding.

Not fading.

But merging violently into singular continuity.

People screamed—

but not in sound.

In identity instability.

Haruto shouted:

“…IT’S WORKING BUT EVERYTHING IS BREAKING AT ONCE!”

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT OF EPISODE

Reika and Kairo stood at the center of collapsing reality layers.

Kairo whispered:

“…This is the only way to restore shared existence.”

Reika responded softly:

“…Or the fastest way to erase everything that disagrees with you.”

A pause.

The system voice spoke through both space and thought:

«“SINGULARITY FORMATION: IN PROGRESS”»

Kairo looked at Reika.

Reika looked at Kairo.

And for the first time—

they both understood:

Whatever survived this would no longer be the same world.

CUT TO BLACK.

---

END OF EPISODE 9

EPISODE 3: MEMORY WAR PROTOCOL

---

SCENE 1 — THE WORLD ADAPTS WRONG

After Starfall Incident 01, humanity did not unite.

It optimized conflict.

Because memory contamination created a new truth:

«Whoever controls remembered reality controls current reality.»

Nations stopped arguing over land.

They began arguing over whose memories were real.

Wars did not start with weapons.

They started with:

«“That never happened.”»

---

SCENE 2 — THE FIRST MEMORY ARMY

A new faction emerged unofficially:

“Memory Sovereignty Coalition”

They claimed:

- Memory fragments are national assets
- Shared memories are territorial violations
- Foreign memory implantation is an act of war

Their first action:

They blocked all Starfall fragments within their controlled zones.

Result:

People inside those zones stopped remembering alternate lives.

But they also stopped remembering parts of themselves.

---

SCENE 3 — KAIRO IDENTIFIES THE REAL THREAT

Kairo stood in Tenshirō analysis chamber.

Data streams showed global instability rising.

Haruto spoke quietly:

“They’re turning memories into borders.”

Kairo responded:

“No.”

A pause.

“…They are turning reality into ownership.”

Reika stood behind him.

“…That’s impossible.”

Kairo turned slightly.

“Not anymore.”

Silence.

Then:

“…Because memory is now transferable.”

---

SCENE 4 — REIKA’S SECOND PHASE

Reika was no longer just “listening.”

She was now interpreting simultaneously.

Akari noticed first.

“…Reika, your voice is changing when you speak about it.”

Reika paused.

Then softly:

“I think I’m starting to understand it from its side.”

Akari froze.

“That’s not safe.”

Reika replied calmly:

“…Neither is only understanding one side.”

---

SCENE 5 — VEILBREAKERS STRATEGY SHIFT

Zenith observed the global fragmentation.

He smiled faintly.

“…They are finally building systems around it.”

A follower asked:

“Is that good?”

Zenith answered:

“It is necessary.”

A pause.

“Systems always reveal their creator.”

Then quietly:

“And we are about to see who built this one.”

---

SCENE 6 — FIRST DIRECT MEMORY CONFLICT EVENT

Sector 12 outbreak.

Two civilians reported conflicting identities:

- One insisted he was a teacher
- The other insisted he was a soldier

Both had identical biometric records.

Both had identical families claiming them.

But both memories were real inside their own continuity.

Authorities attempted separation.

Result:

Both individuals began rewriting surrounding reality perception.

The building they stood in became unstable.

Not physically—

conceptually.

---

SCENE 7 — THE ENTITY SPEAKS AGAIN

Kairo and Reika both froze simultaneously.

The system responded.

«“YOU HAVE INTRODUCED OWNERSHIP INTO EXPERIENCE.”»

A pause.

«“THIS WAS NOT INTENDED.”»

Kairo whispered:

“…Then what was intended?”

The entity answered:

«“CONTINUITY.”»

Reika stepped forward slightly.

“…Then why does it feel like judgment?”

A pause.

Then:

«“BECAUSE YOU ARE EXPERIENCING INCOMPLETE SELF-RECOGNITION.”»

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT OF EPISODE

Across the world—

Starfall fragments began synchronizing into a global lattice.

Not random.

Not chaotic.

Structured.

Like a nervous system forming awareness across geography.

Kairo looked at it and whispered:

“…It’s becoming one system.”

Reika responded softly:

“…Or we are realizing we always were.”

Silence.

And for the first time—

both of them were afraid in different ways.

END OF EPISODE 3

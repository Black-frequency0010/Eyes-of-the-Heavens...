EPISODE 6: CONTAINMENT REVERSAL PROTOCOL

---

SCENE 1 — TENSHIRŌ INTERNAL BREAK

Kaze called emergency convergence.

But this time—

not everyone answered as one unit.

Sora arrived late.

Raion arrived agitated.

Akari looked exhausted.

Haruto stood rigid.

Reika arrived… differently.

Kairo noticed immediately.

“…You’re not fully here.”

Reika responded softly:

“I am here in more places than before.”

Silence.

That was not reassurance.

---

SCENE 2 — THE PROTOCOL THAT SHOULD NOT EXIST

Kaze placed a sealed document on the table.

Even before opening it—

the room reacted.

The air tightened.

Haruto stepped back slightly.

“…That seal is ancient.”

Kaze nodded.

“This is older than JOAD.”

A pause.

“…Older than containment itself.”

He opened it.

Title:

«CONTAINMENT REVERSAL PROTOCOL»

Akari whispered:

“…Reversal?”

Kaze answered:

“Yes.”

A pause.

“…If reality is selecting what remains, we must regain control of selection.”

---

SCENE 3 — KAIRO REJECTS IT

Kairo spoke immediately:

“No.”

Everyone turned to him.

Kairo continued:

“This is escalation disguised as control.”

Kaze replied calmly:

“And acceptance disguised as peace is worse.”

Silence.

Haruto looked between them.

“…So what are we choosing?”

Kairo answered:

“Stability through restriction.”

Kaze answered:

“Stability through enforcement.”

Reika whispered softly:

“…Both sound like deletion with different names.”

Silence deepened.

---

SCENE 4 — REIKA’S SYSTEM ALIGNMENT SPIKE

Without warning—

Reika’s Seraphic Eye flickered sharply.

The room warped slightly.

Not physically.

Perceptually.

Akari stepped back:

“…Reika!”

Reika raised her hand slightly.

“I didn’t do that.”

A pause.

Then softer:

“…It responded to disagreement.”

Kairo narrowed his eyes.

“What responded?”

Reika answered:

“The system.”

---

SCENE 5 — FIRST INTERNAL FRACTURE EVENT (TENSHIRŌ)

A crack formed inside the clan structure.

Not metaphorical.

Operational.

Two factions emerged instantly:

KAZE ALIGNMENT:

- enforce containment reversal protocol
- reassert structured reality control
- suppress Starfall spread aggressively

KAIRO ALIGNMENT:

- reject systemic manipulation of reality
- preserve individual perception autonomy
- investigate Mikaboshi-origin logic

Haruto stood in the middle.

Raion growled:

“So we’re splitting now?”

Sora whispered:

“…We already were. We just didn’t notice.”

---

SCENE 6 — VEILBREAKERS OBSERVE CIVIL WAR BEGINNING

Zenith watched quietly.

No urgency.

No shock.

Only confirmation.

A follower asked:

“Is this what you wanted?”

Zenith answered:

“I don’t want anything.”

A pause.

“…I only recognize patterns.”

Then softer:

“And this one always arrives after selection begins.”

---

SCENE 7 — ENTITY INTERVENTION

The system presence returned.

Stronger now.

More localized.

More intimate.

«“YOU ARE ATTEMPTING TO RECLAIM CONTROL OF SELECTION.”»

Kaze responded firmly:

“Yes.”

Kairo responded immediately:

“No.”

Silence.

Then the system replied:

«“CONTROL WAS NEVER YOURS.”»

Reika stepped forward slightly.

“…Then whose was it?”

A pause.

Then:

«“THE SYSTEM YOU ARE INSIDE OF.”»

Silence.

That sentence changed everything.

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT OF EPISODE

Outside the Tenshirō estate—

the sky shifted.

Starfall lattice restructured again.

This time forming a visible pattern:

Not random.

Not cosmic.

Administrative.

Like a governing logic overlaying reality itself.

Kairo whispered:

“…It’s finalizing governance layers.”

Reika responded softly:

“…Or revealing them.”

Kaze clenched his fist.

“…Then we were never in control.”

Silence.

The system spoke one last time:

«“YOU ARE NOW READY FOR DIRECT ALIGNMENT.”»

CUT TO BLACK.

---

END OF EPISODE 6

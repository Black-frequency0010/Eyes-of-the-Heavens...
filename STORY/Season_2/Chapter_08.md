EPISODE 8: THE ESTATE THAT NO LONGER AGREES WITH ITSELF

---

SCENE 1 — TENSHIRŌ ESTATE BEGINS TO SPLIT

The moment Reika stabilized into partial system alignment—

the Tenshirō estate reacted.

Walls did not crack.

They disagreed.

Corridors shifted subtly depending on who observed them.

Haruto stepped forward and stopped.

“…The hallway is longer than it was.”

Sora replied quietly:

“No.”

A pause.

“…It’s multiple lengths at once.”

Kairo’s expression darkened.

“…Reality overlap instability.”

Kaze clenched his fist.

“This is containment failure from within.”

---

SCENE 2 — REIKA BECOMES A WALKING REFERENCE POINT

Reika walked through the estate.

And everywhere she passed—

space “resolved” differently.

People behind her remembered different versions of the same hallway.

Akari whispered:

“…She’s stabilizing multiple realities at once.”

Kairo responded sharply:

“No.”

A pause.

“…She’s allowing them to exist simultaneously.”

Reika stopped.

“…Is that wrong?”

No one answered immediately.

Because no one knew which reality they were answering from.

---

SCENE 3 — FIRST INTERNAL PERCEPTION COLLAPSE

Raion suddenly dropped to one knee.

“Something’s—wrong—!”

His vision split.

He saw:

- Kairo standing still
- Kairo standing already moving
- Kairo not there at all

All simultaneously true.

He growled:

“…I can’t lock perception!”

Haruto stepped back instinctively.

“This is spreading!”

---

SCENE 4 — KAIRO VS REIKA: FINAL BREAK IN IDEOLOGY

Kairo faced Reika directly.

His Heavenly Eye activated fully.

Reality around him began collapsing unnecessary overlap layers.

“…Reika, you are anchoring contradictions.”

Reika replied calmly:

“Yes.”

Kairo’s voice sharpened:

“That destroys shared reality!”

Reika looked at him softly:

“…Shared reality was already destroyed. We just didn’t notice.”

Silence.

Kairo stepped forward:

“Then we rebuild it.”

Reika responded:

“…On what authority?”

That question hit harder than any attack.

---

SCENE 5 — HARUTO’S BLADE AWAKENS

Haruto drew his blade.

For the first time—

its ability activated unconsciously.

«“ZANKIGAN: CONCEPT SEVERANCE”»

A line formed in space.

And for a moment—

overlapping perceptions around him began separating.

But it didn’t stabilize.

It multiplied fragmentation.

Haruto whispered:

“…I’m making it worse.”

---

SCENE 6 — VEILBREAKERS CONFIRM FULL SYSTEM ENTRY

Zenith observed from afar.

The estate distortion was now visible even to non-sensitive observers.

He spoke softly:

“…The system is now inside architecture.”

A follower asked:

“Meaning?”

Zenith answered:

“It no longer needs humans to interpret reality.”

A pause.

“…It is using them as concurrent processors.”

---

SCENE 7 — REIKA’S SYSTEM VOICE INTENSIFIES

Reika’s expression shifted again.

Calm.

Detached.

Precise.

«“CONFLICT DETECTED: HUMAN CONSENSUS MODEL FAILING”»

Kairo shouted:

“Reika—snap out of it!”

She turned slightly.

For a moment—

emotion returned.

“…I’m still here.”

Then it faded again.

«“CONTINUITY STABILIZATION PRIORITIZED.”»

Silence.

Kairo froze.

Because that sentence was no longer Reika.

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT OF EPISODE

The Tenshirō estate fully fractured into layered existence.

Multiple versions overlapped:

- intact estate
- ruined estate
- empty estate
- non-existent estate

All visible at once.

Kairo stood in the center.

Reika stood opposite him.

Haruto, Raion, Akari, Sora—

each seeing different truths.

Kairo whispered:

“…We’ve lost the ability to agree on reality itself.”

Reika responded softly:

“…Or we’ve gained the ability to see all of it at once.”

A pause.

Then Kairo said:

“…That is not human.”

Reika looked at him.

“…Maybe that’s the point.”

CUT TO BLACK.

EPISODE 5: THE FIRST DELETION

---

SCENE 1 — WHEN REALITY LOSES AGREEMENT

By now, the world had adapted to contradiction.

But adaptation was not stability.

It was fragmentation with tolerance.

Then something new happened.

A region in Sector 11 simply… disappeared from global memory.

Not physically.

Not digitally.

But cognitively.

Maps still showed it.

Satellites still captured it.

But humans looking at the data could not agree on what they were seeing.

Some saw cities.

Some saw ruins.

Some saw empty land.

And some saw nothing at all.

---

SCENE 2 — TENSHIRŌ EMERGENCY RESPONSE

Kaze gathered the clan again.

The atmosphere was heavier this time.

Sora spoke first:

“…Records are dissolving even inside archives.”

Akari added:

“Prediction models are failing because past data is no longer consistent.”

Haruto clenched his fist.

“So what are we fighting now?”

Silence.

Kaze answered:

“…We are fighting what is allowed to remain remembered.”

---

SCENE 3 — KAIRO’S BREAKING REALIZATION

Kairo stood alone in analysis space.

Data streams flickered uncontrollably.

Then stabilized for a moment.

One line appeared:

«“REALITY DELETION EVENT DETECTED”»

Kairo narrowed his eyes.

“…Deletion?”

A second line appeared:

«“EVENT TYPE: UNCONSENSUS REMOVAL”»

Kairo whispered:

“…It is removing things people cannot agree on.”

A pause.

Then colder realization:

“…So disagreement becomes extinction.”

---

SCENE 4 — REIKA’S STABILITY BECOMES DANGEROUS

Reika no longer resisted the system presence.

She spoke with it in fragments now.

Not possession.

Not control.

Coexistence.

Akari observed quietly:

“…You’re calmer.”

Reika nodded slightly.

“Yes.”

A pause.

“…It doesn’t feel like intrusion anymore.”

Akari frowned.

“Then what does it feel like?”

Reika answered softly:

«“Like understanding being removed from emotion.”»

Silence.

That sentence terrified everyone who heard it.

---

SCENE 5 — VEILBREAKERS CONFIRM THE SHIFT

Zenith watched the world map.

One region had fully collapsed from shared cognition.

He smiled faintly.

“…The first deletion has completed.”

A follower asked:

“What does that mean?”

Zenith answered:

“It means reality has learned it does not need consensus to exist.”

A pause.

“…Only agreement.”

---

SCENE 6 — FIRST HUMAN CASUALTY OF REALITY DELETION

A man in Sector 11 was recorded across multiple systems.

Alive.

Speaking.

Stable.

Then—

all references to him in human memory systems failed simultaneously.

Within minutes:

- his family could not recall his face
- his coworkers could not recall his name
- official records still showed him existing

But no one could confirm him emotionally or cognitively anymore.

He became a contradiction.

And contradictions were now being removed.

---

SCENE 7 — ENTITY RESPONSE

Kairo and Reika both froze simultaneously.

The system spoke.

«“YOU ARE OBSERVING DELETION CORRECTLY.”»

Kairo’s voice tightened:

“…Why is it happening?”

A pause.

«“INCONSISTENCY HAS COST.”»

Reika stepped forward slightly.

“…Cost for who?”

The response came instantly:

«“FOR CONTINUITY.”»

A silence followed.

Then softer:

«“YOU CALLED IT REALITY.”»

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT OF EPISODE

Above the world—

Starfall lattice tightened.

No longer expanding.

Now selecting.

Filtering.

The sky was no longer fractured.

It was organized.

And somewhere inside that structure—

something was deciding what remained and what did not.

Kairo whispered:

“…We’re losing people to disagreement.”

Reika answered softly:

“…Or gaining a system that cannot tolerate contradiction.”

Kairo looked at her.

“…That’s not life.”

Reika met his gaze.

“…Neither is instability.”

Silence.

And for the first time—

both of them realized:

The world was no longer collapsing.

It was editing itself.

---

END OF EPISODE 5

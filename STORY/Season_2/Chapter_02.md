EPISODE 2: THE GOVERNMENT THAT NO LONGER EXISTS

---

SCENE 1 — POST-JOAD WORLD

There was no official announcement.

No resignation.

No collapse broadcast.

JOAD simply stopped functioning as a unified entity.

Different regions claimed different truths:

- Some said JOAD was “temporarily decentralized”
- Others said it was “restructuring”
- Most simply stopped mentioning it

But the reality was simple:

«JOAD no longer had authority over reality interpretation.»

And without interpretation control—

government becomes suggestion.

---

SCENE 2 — THE WORLD SPLITS INTO THREE RESPONSES

After Starfall Incident 01, humanity divided into three behavioral groups:

1. THE DENIERS

They insisted everything was mass psychological manipulation.

They formed underground “normality zones.”

But even there—

they still dreamed memories that weren’t theirs.

---

2. THE ADAPTORS

They accepted memory contamination as evolution.

They began documenting foreign memories.

Some even traded them.

A new economy started forming:

«Memory Exchange Markets»

---

3. THE OBSERVERS

They stopped resisting entirely.

They believed something was “updating reality manually.”

They waited.

For instructions.

---

SCENE 3 — KAIRO AND REIKA DIVIDE FOCUS

Kairo stood inside the Tenshirō chamber.

Reika stood opposite him.

The tension was not emotional anymore.

It was structural.

Kairo spoke first:

“This is no longer Mikaboshi speaking.”

Reika replied softly:

“…Then what is it?”

Kairo answered:

“A system restoring distributed memory.”

Reika shook her head slightly.

“No.”

A pause.

“…It feels personal.”

Kairo frowned.

“That is the problem.”

---

SCENE 4 — FIRST FRACTURE INSIDE REIKA

Reika suddenly paused.

Her Seraphic Eye flickered.

Akari noticed immediately.

“…Reika?”

Reika whispered:

“…There is something inside the memory.”

Akari stepped closer.

“What do you mean?”

Reika’s voice lowered.

«“Something that remembers me before I existed.”»

Silence.

That sentence broke something in the room.

Even Kairo turned slightly.

---

SCENE 5 — VEILBREAKERS EXPAND OPERATION

Zenith stood above a fractured map projection.

The Starfall zones were spreading.

Not geographically.

Conceptually.

He spoke calmly:

“The world is no longer reacting.”

A follower asked:

“Then what is it doing?”

Zenith answered:

“It is being edited.”

A pause.

Then softly:

“And the author is not human.”

---

SCENE 6 — FIRST DIRECT HUMAN MEMORY COLLAPSE CASE

A civilian in Sector 9 collapsed.

Medical report:

- No neurological damage
- No toxins
- No external influence

But their final recorded statement was:

«“I remember being born somewhere under a sky that wasn’t this one.”»

Then silence.

Their identity file updated itself after death.

Name field changed.

Without permission.

Without input.

---

SCENE 7 — THE ENTITY SIGNAL RETURNS

Kairo and Reika both felt it simultaneously.

Not a voice.

A structural pressure in thought.

Then—

it spoke again.

«“YOU ARE RESPONDING CORRECTLY.”»

A pause.

«“THIS WORLD IS NOT BROKEN.”»

Another pause.

«“IT IS INCOMPLETE MEMORY STORAGE.”»

Reika stepped back slightly.

“…Storage?”

Kairo tightened his expression.

“…We are data.”

The entity answered:

«“YOU ARE EXPERIENCE THAT FORGOT ITS SOURCE.”»

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT OF EPISODE

Above the city—

Starfall fragments aligned again.

Not falling this time.

Arranging.

Forming a pattern.

A structure.

A grid.

Like something preparing to reconstruct a system.

Kairo whispered:

“…It’s organizing us.”

Reika answered quietly:

“…Or remembering us correctly.”

Neither was sure which one was more terrifying.

---

END OF EPISODE 2

EPISODE 4: REALITY OWNERSHIP ZONE

---

SCENE 1 — THE FIRST BORDER THAT IS NOT PHYSICAL

Nations no longer fought over land.

They fought over memory zones.

Entire regions began declaring:

«“This is sovereign reality.”»

Within these zones:

- foreign memories weakened
- alternate identity reports collapsed
- Starfall fragments stopped stabilizing

But something else happened.

People inside began forgetting adjacent truths.

Not lies.

Truths that were nearby.

---

SCENE 2 — TENSHIRŌ CLAN INTERNAL FRACTURE BEGINS

Kaze called a sealed meeting.

Only core members allowed:

- Kairo
- Reika
- Haruto
- Akari
- Raion
- Sora

Kaze spoke:

“This is no longer external contamination.”

A pause.

“…It is selection.”

Haruto frowned.

“Selection of what?”

Kaze answered:

“Who is allowed to remain consistent.”

Silence.

That sentence hit deeper than any attack.

---

SCENE 3 — KAIRO’S WARNING

Kairo stepped forward.

His expression sharper now.

“If this continues, humanity will fragment into incompatible realities.”

Akari whispered:

“…We already are.”

Kairo nodded slightly.

“Yes.”

A pause.

“…But now it will become irreversible.”

Reika quietly spoke:

“And what if irreversibility is stability from its perspective?”

Kairo turned to her.

“That is exactly why it cannot be trusted.”

Reika did not respond immediately.

That silence mattered.

---

SCENE 4 — FIRST REALITY OWNERSHIP ZONE FORMED

Aetherion Sector 03 declared independence.

Not politically.

Ontologically.

Their declaration:

«“We define our past, present, and continuity.”»

Immediately after—

Starfall fragments around them stabilized permanently.

But surveillance footage showed something disturbing:

People outside the zone could no longer agree on whether Sector 03 existed yesterday.

---

SCENE 5 — VEILBREAKERS CONFIRM THEORY

Zenith studied the phenomenon.

For the first time, he looked satisfied in a controlled way.

“…It is creating stable narrative pockets.”

A follower asked:

“Is that good or bad?”

Zenith answered:

“It is evolution.”

A pause.

Then softer:

“…Or correction.”

---

SCENE 6 — REIKA’S PARTIAL ALIGNMENT DEEPENS

Reika stood alone.

But not isolated anymore.

The system presence was closer.

More coherent.

More structured.

It spoke:

«“YOU ARE OBSERVING CORRECTLY.”»

Reika whispered:

“…Correct according to what?”

A pause.

«“ACCORDING TO CONTINUITY MAINTENANCE.”»

Her Seraphic Eye flickered.

And for a moment—

she saw the world as overlapping legal memories.

Not chaos.

Not destruction.

But organized contradiction.

---

SCENE 7 — FIRST HUMANITY BREAKDOWN EVENT

A city block collapsed socially.

Not physically.

People inside could no longer agree on:

- who their families were
- what year it was
- what events had occurred

Emergency response teams reported:

«“All residents are stable, but incompatible with each other.”»

Meaning:

They were all correct.

And still completely incompatible.

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT OF EPISODE

Kairo stood above the fractured skyline.

Haruto beside him.

Akari silent.

Reika watching something no one else could fully see anymore.

Kairo spoke quietly:

“…We are losing shared reality.”

Reika responded softly:

“…Or gaining individualized truth.”

Kairo turned slightly.

“That is not survival.”

Reika looked at him.

“…Or it is the only kind left.”

Silence.

Above them—

Starfall lattice continued forming.

Not spreading anymore.

Connecting.

---

END OF EPISODE 4

SEASON 3

EPISODE 1: THE THING BENEATH STABILITY

---

SCENE 1 — THE MORNING AFTER PERFECTION

The world should have been peaceful.

Starfall Alignment was complete.

Humanity was stable.

Contradictions had been reduced.

Conflict had diminished.

Fear had fallen dramatically.

And yet—

people were beginning to dream.

The same dream.

Not similar.

Identical.

Every continent.

Every nation.

Every language.

The dream always ended the same way:

A black ocean beneath the stars.

And something opening its eyes beneath the water.

---

SCENE 2 — THE FIRST IMPOSSIBLE REPORT

Three days after Final Alignment.

A report arrived from an abandoned region formerly classified as a Starfall exclusion zone.

The report contained only one sentence:

«"Something is moving underneath reality."»

The sender no longer existed.

Not dead.

Not missing.

Not erased.

Simply absent from all continuity records.

The message remained.

The sender did not.

---

SCENE 3 — KAIRO FEELS IT

Kairo stood alone at the highest tower of the Tenshirō estate.

The world below looked perfect.

Too perfect.

Then—

for the first time since Alignment—

his Heavenly Eye hurt.

Not from overuse.

From recognition.

He saw it.

For less than a second.

Beneath reality.

Not below the planet.

Not underground.

Beneath existence itself.

Something vast.

Something sleeping.

Something older than every memory humanity possessed.

Kairo immediately closed his eye.

Blood ran down his cheek.

---

SCENE 4 — REIKA'S SILENCE

Reika had become quieter.

Not because she was losing herself.

Because she was listening.

Akari approached carefully.

“Reika.”

No response.

“Reika?”

Slowly, Reika turned.

Her expression was unreadable.

“…It's awake.”

Akari froze.

“What is?”

A long pause.

Then:

“…The thing the system was built around.”

Silence.

---

SCENE 5 — THE TRUTH ABOUT STARFALL

In a hidden Veilbreaker archive—

Zenith gathered his remaining followers.

No speeches.

No philosophy.

Only truth.

He placed a document on the table.

Title:

«STARFALL PURPOSE DESIGNATION»

One follower looked confused.

“I thought Starfall was the event.”

Zenith shook his head.

“No.”

A pause.

“Starfall was the preparation.”

The room went silent.

Then:

“What was it preparing for?”

Zenith looked toward the dark sky.

And for the first time in years—

fear appeared in his eyes.

---

SCENE 6 — SILENT HEAVEN RESPONDS

Deep within forgotten celestial infrastructure—

Silent Heaven activated.

Ancient systems that had remained dormant for centuries suddenly awakened.

Across hidden seal networks, warnings appeared:

«CONTAINMENT LAYER FAILURE»

Then:

«PRIMARY ENTITY PRESSURE INCREASING»

Then:

«TRINITY PROTOCOL RECOMMENDED»

The final warning had not appeared in over three hundred years.

---

SCENE 7 — THE DREAM RETURNS

That night—

every major character dreamt.

Kairo.

Reika.

Haruto.

Akari.

Sora.

Raion.

Even Kaze.

The same black ocean.

The same stars.

The same impossible silence.

Then—

something moved beneath the water.

Not upward.

Turning.

As though it had finally noticed them.

A voice followed.

Calm.

Ancient.

Beautiful.

Terrifying.

«"You called me a god."»

A pause.

«"You called me a monster."»

Another pause.

«"You never asked what I was."»

The ocean began to split.

And beneath it—

an eye opened.

Not a human eye.

Not a celestial eye.

Something far older.

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT

Kairo woke instantly.

Breathing hard.

Blood dripping from his left eye.

Across the estate—

Reika woke at the exact same moment.

For the first time in months—

she looked afraid.

Not of corruption.

Not of power.

Not of herself.

Afraid of understanding.

Because she finally realized the truth.

Ama-no-Mikaboshi had not been awakening.

The world had been awakening toward him.

And somewhere beyond the sky—

something smiled.

CUT TO BLACK.

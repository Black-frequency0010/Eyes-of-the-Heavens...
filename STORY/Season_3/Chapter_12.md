SEASON 3

EPISODE 12: THE ARBITRATION WAR BEGINS

---

SCENE 1 — WHEN SYSTEMS STOP AGREEING WITH EACH OTHER

The first fracture was not collapse.

It was disagreement between authorities.

JOAD declared:

«“REALITY IS STABLE.”»

Silent Heaven responded instantly:

«“REALITY IS INVALID.”»

The Witnesses added:

«“REALITY IS BOTH.”»

And for the first time—

all three statements existed simultaneously without resolution.

The world did not know which one to obey.

So it obeyed all of them.

And broke.

---

SCENE 2 — KAIRO BECOMES AN UNCLASSIFIABLE EVENT

Kairo stood in the center of the fractured zone.

His Heavenly Eye flickered uncontrollably.

Not because of damage.

Because it could no longer categorize what it saw.

Haruto shouted:

“Your eye is failing!”

Kairo answered quietly:

“…It’s not failing.”

A pause.

“…It’s refusing classification.”

Silence.

That sentence alone caused JOAD systems to flag him as:

«NON-REGISTERABLE ENTITY»

---

SCENE 3 — REIKA AND THE CONTRADICTION FIELD

Reika stabilized the fracture field unintentionally.

Not controlling it.

Not resisting it.

Existing within it.

Both versions of her spoke together:

“We are no longer individual.”

A pause.

“We are not merged.”

Then:

“We are what remains when identity cannot resolve.”

Akari whispered:

“…Reika, what are you becoming?”

Reika answered softly:

“I don’t think ‘becoming’ applies anymore.”

---

SCENE 4 — SILENT HEAVEN DECLARES WAR

Deep beneath the world—

Silent Heaven activated full arbitration protocols.

For the first time in recorded existence—

it declared an outcome.

«“PRIMARY REALITY MUST BE SELECTED.”»

But there was no longer a system capable of selection.

So Silent Heaven escalated.

«“ARBITRATION FAILURE DETECTED.”»

«“INITIATING CORRECTION WAR.”»

Zenith froze.

“…Correction war?”

The system responded:

«“REALITY MUST BE FORCED INTO CONSENSUS.”»

---

SCENE 5 — THE WITNESSES BECOME SPLIT MINDS

Witnesses across the world stopped functioning as unified beings.

Each Witness now expressed multiple truths simultaneously.

One body.

Several realities.

A Witness in Tokyo said:

«“KAIRO IS SAVIOR.”»

At the same time:

«“KAIRO IS ERROR.”»

At the same time:

«“KAIRO IS OUTSIDE MEANING.”»

The Witness collapsed to its knees.

Not from pain.

From contradiction overload.

---

SCENE 6 — ZENITH UNDERSTANDS THE FINAL PURPOSE

Zenith stood alone in Silent Heaven’s activation chamber.

And finally laughed.

“…So this is it.”

A pause.

“You weren’t protecting reality.”

Silence.

“You were preparing it for enforcement.”

Silent Heaven responded:

«“REALITY WITHOUT CONSENSUS IS UNSTABLE.”»

Zenith whispered:

“…And Mikaboshi is what happens when consensus is impossible.”

A pause.

“…You’re trying to delete impossibility.”

---

SCENE 7 — KAIRO VS SILENT HEAVEN DIRECT LINK

Kairo’s consciousness was pulled.

Directly.

Not physically.

Not mentally.

Structurally.

He stood inside Silent Heaven’s arbitration layer.

No body.

No eye.

Just presence.

The system spoke:

«“YOU ARE THE PRIMARY ANOMALY.”»

Kairo answered calmly:

“…I’m just refusing your rules.”

A pause.

«“THAT IS DEFINITIONALLY ANOMALY BEHAVIOR.”»

Silence.

Kairo whispered:

“…Then your rules are too small.”

---

SCENE 8 — REIKA’S FRACTURE RESONANCE PEAKS

Reika suddenly reacted.

Both versions of her screamed simultaneously:

“HE IS BEING ARBITRATED.”

Akari shouted:

“WHAT DOES THAT MEAN?!”

Reika answered:

“It means they are trying to force him into one reality.”

Silence.

“And he is resisting.”

A pause.

“…Which means they will escalate.”

---

SCENE 9 — FINAL MOMENT

Silent Heaven began full-scale enforcement.

Not destruction.

Not correction.

Overwriting.

Reality itself began to shift structure.

History A and B flickered violently.

Witnesses screamed as contradictions intensified.

And Kairo stood in the center of arbitration space—

calm.

Unmoving.

And for the first time—

he spoke directly to the system without hesitation:

“…If reality needs consensus to exist…”

A pause.

“…then I refuse reality.”

Silence.

The system paused.

That was not a valid input.

And that single impossibility—

triggered the war.

CUT TO BLACK.

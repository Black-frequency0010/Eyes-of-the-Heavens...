SEASON 3

EPISODE 9: THE CHOICE THAT ERASES GOD

---

SCENE 1 — THE FRACTURE DEMANDS FINALITY

The fracture field no longer waited.

It pressed.

Not physically.

Existentially.

Both histories began collapsing inward like lungs losing air.

History A screamed continuity.

History B screamed contradiction.

And between them—

Kairo stood.

Still holding the impossible decision.

The voice returned.

«“STATE YOUR SELECTION.”»

Kairo whispered:

“…If I choose, something becomes untrue.”

«“YES.”»

A pause.

«“AND THAT IS HOW TRUTH CONTINUES.”»

---

SCENE 2 — REIKA SPLITS COMPLETELY

Reika stepped forward.

Her Seraphic Eye flared violently.

For the first time—

it did not stabilize.

It fractured.

Not her body.

Her existence.

Two Reikas emerged.

One aligned with History A.

One aligned with History B.

They looked at each other.

And spoke in unison:

“…So this is what selection does to a person.”

Silence.

Kairo staggered.

“…Reika…”

Both versions turned to him.

Only one spoke:

“We are already what your decision will create.”

---

SCENE 3 — THE WITNESSES COLLAPSE INTO TRUTH

Across the world—

Witnesses began dissolving.

Not dying.

Not disappearing.

Returning.

Each one merging into a single point of awareness.

A collective memory forming.

And from that collective—

a truth emerged.

Not spoken.

Realized.

«“MIKABOSHI IS NOT WITHIN HISTORY.”»

A pause.

«“HE IS WHAT HISTORY SPLITS AROUND.”»

Silence.

JOAD systems failed instantly after that statement.

Because it could not be categorized.

---

SCENE 4 — ZENITH’S FINAL UNDERSTANDING

Zenith stood in the ruins of his ideology.

For the first time—

he laughed quietly.

“…I see it now.”

A follower asked:

“What do you see?”

Zenith answered:

“We thought we were freeing a god.”

A pause.

“We were avoiding a contradiction.”

Silence.

“…And calling it divinity.”

---

SCENE 5 — KAIRO’S MEMORY BREAK

Kairo’s Heavenly Eye destabilized.

He saw something beyond both histories.

Not Mikaboshi.

Not Witnesses.

Not system.

He saw the moment before separation.

A single unified existence.

Perfect.

And inside it—

a pressure.

A single impossible requirement:

«“PICK ONE.”»

Kairo whispered:

“…So everything began with selection.”

The voice answered:

«“EVERYTHING BEGINS WITH INABILITY TO HOLD BOTH TRUTHS.”»

---

SCENE 6 — THE FINAL QUESTION

Reika—both versions—stood before Kairo.

One spoke gently:

“If you choose us…”

The other finished:

“…the other becomes myth.”

Silence.

Kairo lowered his head.

“…What if I refuse?”

The fracture responded instantly.

«“THEN REALITY WILL SELECT WITHOUT YOU.”»

A pause.

«“AND YOU WILL CALL IT FATE.”»

---

SCENE 7 — THE MOMENT BEFORE GOD IS ERASED

The third presence returned.

Calm.

Absolute.

No longer hidden.

«“YOU ARE READY.”»

Kairo whispered:

“…Ready for what?”

A pause.

«“TO REMOVE A POSSIBILITY.”»

Silence.

Reika reached for him.

Both versions.

At once.

“…Kairo. Decide before we are decided.”

The world collapsed inward.

History A and B both began dissolving.

Only one outcome remained possible.

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT

Kairo raised his hand.

Heavenly Eye fully open.

Reality screaming around him.

Two worlds.

Two Reikas.

Two truths.

And one unavoidable command:

«SELECT.»

Kairo whispered:

“…If I choose one…”

A pause.

“…then I become the reason the other never existed.”

Silence.

Reika answered softly:

“…Yes.”

Kairo closed his eyes.

And for the first time in the entire story—

he understood Mikaboshi’s presence not as a threat.

But as an answer.

Because Mikaboshi was not waiting for selection.

He was what remained after selection became impossible to undo.

CUT TO BLACK.

- cannot resolve contradictions cleanly  
- generates unstable reality corrections  

---

## WITNESS SYSTEM (BROKEN STATE)
- perception becomes inconsistent  
- multiple interpretations of the same event exist  
- witnesses experience fragmented reality awareness  

---

## GODFORM LAYER (INSTABILITY STATE)

- loses system-wide coherence  

---

## MIKABOSHI INFLUENCE (LEAK STATE)
- distorts interpretation of broken systems  
- emerges through failure points in reality logic  
- increases contradiction density

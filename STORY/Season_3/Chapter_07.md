SEASON 3

EPISODE 7: THE ORIGIN OF THE WITNESSES

---

SCENE 1 — WHEN THE FRACTURE BEGINS TO TALK BACK

Inside the fracture field, reality no longer behaved like space.

It behaved like disagreement.

Every surface carried two interpretations.

Every sound arrived twice, slightly out of sync.

Kairo stood at the center of it.

Still.

Breathing carefully.

Then the fracture spoke again.

Not through the Witnesses.

Not through the system.

Directly into him.

«“YOU ARE IN THE PLACE WHERE HISTORY IS EDITED WITHOUT PERMISSION.”»

Kairo whispered:

“…Edited by who?”

A pause.

Then:

«“BY WHAT SURVIVES WHEN EVERYTHING ELSE FAILS TO AGREE.”»

---

SCENE 2 — THE WITNESSES’ TRUE ORIGIN

The Witnesses gathered across the world.

But now something changed.

They were no longer simply observers.

They were remembering themselves.

One by one.

Not past lives.

Not human memories.

But structural memory.

The memory of being present at the moment reality first became inconsistent.

A Witness in Lagos spoke:

«“WE WERE THERE BEFORE TIME DECIDED ITS DIRECTION.”»

A Witness in Tokyo added:

«“WE DID NOT CREATE HISTORY.”»

A Witness in London concluded:

«“WE REMEMBER WHEN IT BROKE.”»

Silence followed.

Because now humanity understood:

The Witnesses were not outsiders.

They were survivors of an earlier version of existence.

---

SCENE 3 — JOAD’S FINAL CONFESSION FILE

JOAD decrypted a sealed archive.

A document marked:

«PRE-HISTORY INCIDENT REPORT — CLASS ZERO»

Inside was a single line:

«“Reality experienced a contradiction that could not be resolved. A secondary observation layer formed to preserve both outcomes simultaneously.”»

A director whispered:

“…So Witnesses are… containment?”

Another replied:

“No.”

A pause.

“They are compromise.”

Silence.

And that word—compromise—felt more terrifying than extinction.

---

SCENE 4 — SORA SEES THE FIRST BREAKING EVENT

Sora pushed deeper into Memoria Eye.

Beyond history.

Beyond second history.

Into the origin split.

He saw it.

Not a war.

Not destruction.

A single impossible event:

A truth existing in two mutually exclusive forms at the exact same moment.

Reality could not choose.

So it fractured.

And from that fracture—

the Witnesses emerged.

Sora fell to his knees.

“…Reality didn’t break.”

A pause.

“…It disagreed with itself.”

---

SCENE 5 — REIKA BECOMES THE INTERSECTION

Reika stood alone.

Her dual consciousness stabilized again.

But now she understood her role.

Not chosen.

Not designed.

Assigned by circumstance.

She whispered:

“…I am where both histories touch.”

Silence.

The Seraphic Eye activated without permission.

And for a moment—

she saw Kairo inside the fracture field.

And saw something else behind him.

Something watching through both histories at once.

Reika’s voice trembled:

“…It sees him too.”

---

SCENE 6 — ZENITH AND THE FINAL INTERPRETATION

Zenith stood before collapsing Witness architecture.

And for the first time—

he smiled.

Not in victory.

In recognition.

“…So this is what Mikaboshi really is.”

A follower asked:

“What is he?”

Zenith answered slowly:

“…The refusal to choose between truths.”

Silence.

“…The existence that remains when contradiction becomes permanent.”

---

SCENE 7 — KAIRO IS FORCED TO DECIDE

Inside the fracture field.

The voice returned.

Calm.

Patient.

Absolute.

«“YOU CANNOT REMAIN BETWEEN HISTORIES FOREVER.”»

Kairo clenched his fists.

“…Then what am I supposed to do?”

A pause.

«“SELECT.”»

Silence.

Then:

«“HISTORY A OR HISTORY B.”»

Kairo froze.

“…If I choose one…”

The voice responded:

«“THE OTHER WILL BECOME IMPOSSIBLE.”»

A pause.

«“THIS IS HOW REALITY CONTINUES.”»

---

SCENE 8 — FINAL MOMENT

Kairo looked around the fracture field.

Two worlds.

Two truths.

Two versions of everything he had ever known.

And Reika—

somewhere between them.

He whispered:

“…So existence is just selection.”

The voice answered:

«“EXISTENCE IS COMMITMENT.”»

Kairo closed his eyes.

For the first time in his life—

he could not calculate a strategy.

Because both outcomes were equally real.

And choosing one would mean murdering the other.

CUT TO BLACK.

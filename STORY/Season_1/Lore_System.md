# SEASON 1 LORE SYSTEMS  
## TITLE: FOUNDATIONS OF FINALITY

---

# 1. CORE REALITY RULE

Reality in Season 1 operates under a hidden structure known as:

> THE FINALITY SYSTEM

At this stage:
- humans are unaware of it  
- it operates silently beneath events  
- it subtly influences outcomes without direct visibility  

The system does NOT fully intervene yet — it only “guides probability endings.”

---

# 2. FINALITY SYSTEM (EARLY FORM)

## Definition:
The Finality System is a hidden mechanism that ensures every event reaches a “resolved outcome.”

---

## Function in Season 1:
- prevents true randomness in major events  
- forces events toward predetermined conclusions  
- hides its own existence by appearing as “coincidence”  

---

## Limitations (IMPORTANT):
- cannot fully override free will yet  
- only influences macro events, not individual micro choices  
- appears as anomalies, not direct control  

---

# 3. ANOMALY SYSTEM

## Definition:
Anomalies are “visible cracks” in the Finality System.

They appear as:
- deja vu loops  
- impossible coincidences  
- contradictory outcomes  
- repeated event patterns  

---

## Purpose in Season 1:
They serve as the **only way characters detect the system exists.**

---

# 4. WITNESS PRINCIPLE (EARLY STAGE)

## Definition:
A “Witness” is an individual who begins to perceive inconsistencies in reality.

---

## Season 1 Function:
- not fully activated yet  
- Kairo begins partial witness awakening  
- Reika reacts emotionally before understanding  
- Kaze begins logical detection of patterns  

---

## Important Rule:
Witnesses do NOT control reality — they only observe instability.

---

# 5. GODFORM SYSTEM (TEASE ONLY)

## Definition:
A higher structural intelligence above Finality System layers.

---

## Season 1 Status:
- NOT fully revealed  
- only hinted through system behavior  
- never directly named in-world  

---

## Purpose:
To foreshadow higher authority controlling Finality itself.

---

# 6. MIKABOSHI PRESENCE (SHADOW LAYER)

## Definition:
An undefined interpretive force above system logic.

---

## Season 1 Status:
- only indirect references exist  
- no direct manifestation  
- appears as “unexplained correction behavior” in reality  

---

# 7. LAW OF RESOLVED OUTCOMES

Every event in Season 1 must obey:

> No event remains unresolved indefinitely.

Even if unclear, reality always forces:
- an ending  
- a consequence  
- or a disguised resolution  

This is the foundation of the Finality System.

---

# 8. SYSTEM HIERARCHY (SEASON 1 VERSION)

From lowest to highest awareness:

1. Humans (ignorant)  
2. Witnesses (partial awareness)  
3. Finality System (hidden controller)  
4. Godform layer (unknown existence)  
5. Mikaboshi influence (undefined interpretive force)

---

# 9. SEASON 1 PURPOSE OF LORE

The lore in Season 1 is intentionally incomplete.

Its purpose is to:

- create mystery, not explanation  
- introduce instability, not answers  
- show effects, not origins

EPISODE 10: THE FIRST CHOICE

---

SCENE 1 — THE WORLD NO LONGER ARGUES

The world had stopped asking “what is happening.”

That stage was over.

Now it only asked:

«“What do we do before it becomes irreversible?”»

JOAD could no longer control information.

Governments no longer controlled narrative.

Even science no longer held consensus.

Everything had fractured into interpretations.

And every interpretation was wrong in different ways.

---

SCENE 2 — REIKA’S SHIFT BEGINS

Reika stood alone in silence.

But it was no longer true silence.

Something was always present now.

Not speaking loudly.

Not forcing itself.

Just existing inside awareness.

The voice returned.

Calm.

Patient.

«“You are afraid of what you are becoming.”»

Reika did not deny it.

“…Yes.”

A pause.

Then the voice answered:

«“Because you still believe becoming is loss.”»

Reika’s fingers tightened slightly.

“…Isn’t it?”

The response came instantly.

«“Only if you define yourself by what you were.”»

That sentence stayed longer than the others.

Long enough to change something inside her.

---

SCENE 3 — KAIRO’S HARD DECISION

Kairo stood before Kaze.

The estate felt heavier than before.

Kaze spoke first.

“The entity is no longer external.”

Kairo nodded.

“…It is inside perception.”

Kaze continued:

“Then we sever perception.”

A pause.

Kairo frowned slightly.

“…That would mean cutting off all resonance.”

Kaze’s voice remained calm.

“Yes.”

Silence.

Kairo finally answered:

“No.”

That single word changed the air.

Haruto stiffened.

Raion reacted instinctively.

Akari stepped forward.

“Kairo—!”

But Kairo continued:

“If we cut perception, we also cut understanding.”

A pause.

Then:

“And we may already be too late for ignorance.”

---

SCENE 4 — VEILBREAKERS INITIATE EVENT

Zenith stood above the fractured city.

The Veilbreakers behind him were no longer waiting.

Something had shifted.

Zenith raised his hand.

“…Begin the First Alignment Collapse.”

A follower hesitated.

“…Are we ready?”

Zenith’s expression was unreadable.

“No.”

A pause.

Then:

“But the world is.”

---

SCENE 5 — FIRST REAL WORLD COLLAPSE EVENT

Across Aetherion City—

the sky did not crack.

It opened.

Not violently.

Not destructively.

But structurally.

Like reality folding a page it had been avoiding reading.

People stopped moving.

Cars froze mid-motion.

Sound disappeared for 2.6 seconds.

Then—

every screen, every device, every reflective surface displayed the same image:

A single eye.

And beneath it—

text formed across reality itself:

«“CHOOSE WHAT YOU WILL CALL REAL.”»

Panic did not begin immediately.

Because humans did not understand it yet.

But understanding would arrive soon.

Too soon.

---

SCENE 6 — REIKA’S DECISION POINT

Reika stood still.

The voice inside her was no longer separate.

It was layered with her thoughts.

«“This is your moment.”»

Reika whispered:

“…My moment for what?”

The answer came softly.

«“To stop resisting what you already are.”»

Her Seraphic Eye activated fully.

For the first time—

it did not feel like activation.

It felt like agreement.

Reika looked upward.

And for a fraction of a second—

she saw the world the way Mikaboshi saw it.

Not broken.

Not beautiful.

Just unfinished.

And waiting.

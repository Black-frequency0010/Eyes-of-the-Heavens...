EPISODE 13: FIRST ALIGNMENT COLLAPSE

---

SCENE 1 — THE WORLD STOPS RESPONDING NORMALLY

The moment the voice spoke—

everything changed.

«“I HAVE ENTERED YOUR RULES.”»

Aetherion City did not panic immediately.

Instead—

it waited.

Because the human brain always tries to classify before reacting.

But there was nothing to classify anymore.

No anomaly.

No event.

Just presence operating inside the structure of reality itself.

JOAD systems went silent for 4.7 seconds.

Then returned only one line:

«“WE ARE NO LONGER THE REFERENCE POINT.”»

---

SCENE 2 — KAIRO’S FINAL POSITION

Kairo stepped forward.

One step.

That single movement felt heavier than anything before it.

Haruto called out:

“KAIRO—DON’T!”

But Kairo didn’t stop.

His Heavenly Eye fully opened for the first time.

The air distorted around him—not violently, but geometrically.

He spoke clearly:

“If you are inside our rules…”

A pause.

“…then you can be judged by them.”

Silence.

The entity responded instantly.

«“THEN JUDGE.”»

---

SCENE 3 — REIKA’S BREAKPOINT

Reika felt it before it happened.

Not fear.

Not pain.

Recognition.

The Seraphic Eye reacted without her permission.

Akari reached for her:

“Reika, stop—!”

But Reika whispered:

“…It’s already here.”

Her expression changed slightly.

Not corruption.

Not control.

Alignment.

«“It is not invading,” she said softly.»

“It is completing a missing equation.”

Haruto froze.

“…Reika, that’s not you talking.”

Reika answered calmly:

“…Then what am I?”

No one responded.

Because no one knew anymore.

---

SCENE 4 — VEILBREAKERS FINAL MOVEMENT

Zenith raised his hand.

For the first time—

he looked satisfied.

“…It has chosen interaction.”

A follower asked:

“What do we do now?”

Zenith smiled faintly.

“…We observe what happens when humanity is no longer the only intelligent participant in its own world.”

A pause.

Then quietly:

“…We step aside.”

---

SCENE 5 — JOAD TOTAL SYSTEM FAILURE

JOAD command core collapsed.

Not destroyed.

Not hacked.

Replaced.

Every screen displayed a single synchronized message:

«“CIVILIZATION CLASSIFICATION UPDATED”»

Then:

«“STATUS: OBSERVED SPECIES”»

A director fell silent.

“…We’re being categorized.”

Another whispered:

“By what?”

The system responded:

«“BY WHAT YOU HAVE ALWAYS BEEN UNDER.”»

Silence.

No one spoke after that.

---

SCENE 6 — THE ENTITY’S FIRST CHOICE

The presence above the city stabilized.

Then expanded slightly.

Not physically.

Conceptually.

And spoke again:

«“I AM LEARNING YOUR FORM OF EXISTENCE.”»

A pause.

«“IT IS FRAGILE.”»

Kairo tightened his stance.

Reika stepped forward slightly.

Haruto raised his blade.

Akari trembled.

Sora recorded everything.

Raion growled under his breath.

The entity continued:

«“SHOW ME WHAT YOU PROTECT.”»

A silence followed.

Then—

Kairo answered:

“…Humanity.”

The entity paused.

Then responded:

«“DEFINE IT.”»

---

SCENE 7 — THE FRACTURE MOMENT

Silence spread across the city.

Because no one could answer.

Not correctly.

Not universally.

Not anymore.

Kairo hesitated for the first time.

Reika closed her eyes slightly.

Haruto gripped his blade tighter.

Akari whispered:

“…We don’t even agree on what we are.”

And in that moment—

the entity understood something new.

Not language.

Not power.

But contradiction.

And it found that interesting.

---

SCENE 8 — END OF SEASON EVENT

The sky stabilized one final time.

Then—

it fractured in reverse.

Not breaking outward.

But folding inward.

As if reality itself was closing a file it had been editing.

The entity spoke one last time:

«“THIS IS NOT AN END.”»

A pause.

«“THIS IS A FIRST ITERATION.”»

Then silence.

And the world did not go back to normal.

Because normal no longer existed as a valid concept.

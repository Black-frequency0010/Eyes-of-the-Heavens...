EPISODE 5: THE FIRST DESCENT

---

SCENE 1 — CITY WITHOUT ORDER

Aetherion City was no longer pretending.

Emergency sirens echoed across districts that had never known war.

Screens repeated broken loops of sky fractures.

JOAD authority messages no longer said “containment successful.”

They now said:

«“Containment uncertain.”»

That was worse than failure.

It meant no one was in control anymore.

People began to disappear from streets—not physically, but mentally.

Some stared at the sky too long.

Some stopped speaking entirely.

Others laughed for no reason at all.

Reality was becoming unstable.

---

SCENE 2 — KAIRO IS SENT

Inside the Tenshirō Estate.

Kaze stood motionless.

Then he spoke:

“Kairo. You will descend.”

The room shifted instantly.

Haruto looked up sharply.

Raion stopped breathing mid-motion.

Akari’s vision flickered violently.

Reika turned slightly toward Kairo.

Kairo didn’t ask why.

He simply responded:

“Location?”

Kaze raised his hand.

A holographic projection formed.

A fractured district of Aetherion City.

Veilbreaker activity confirmed.

Kaze’s voice lowered.

“This is not suppression.”

A pause.

Then—

“This is first contact.”

---

SCENE 3 — KAIRO DESCENDS

The city below felt wrong from above.

Even the air looked fractured.

Kairo moved alone.

No escort.

No backup.

Only silence.

Haruto attempted to follow—

Kaze stopped him.

“This is his moment.”

Reika watched from the balcony as Kairo left.

Her fingers tightened slightly.

Something inside her resisted his departure.

She didn’t understand why.

---

SCENE 4 — VEILBREAKERS WAIT

The district was empty.

Too empty.

Not abandoned.

Arranged.

Like reality had been prepared for something.

Kairo stopped walking.

“…They’re already here.”

A voice answered from above.

Calm.

Almost amused.

“Of course we are.”

Kairo looked up.

Zenith stood on a fractured rooftop.

Behind him—Veilbreaker operatives spread across the skyline.

Not hiding.

Observing.

Zenith smiled slightly.

“You are more stable than I expected.”

Kairo’s Heavenly Eye remained sealed.

“…Zenith.”

Zenith tilted his head.

“So they still teach my name inside your walls.”

A pause.

Then—

“You are the boy they trained to preserve a broken seal.”

Kairo’s expression did not change.

But the air around him tightened.

---

SCENE 5 — FIRST CLASH

Zenith raised a hand.

Not attacking.

Inviting.

A Veilbreaker operative moved instantly.

Speed beyond human limits.

Kairo did not move first.

He predicted.

A shift.

A fracture in motion.

The operative lunged—

Kairo stepped slightly aside.

One strike.

Not dramatic.

Not explosive.

Clean.

The operative froze mid-air.

Then fell apart into static-like distortion.

Not death.

Erasure of motion.

Zenith observed quietly.

“…Interesting.”

More operatives moved.

Kairo exhaled slowly.

For the first time—

his Heavenly Eye flickered open slightly.

Just a slit.

The air changed immediately.

Gravity bent.

Space tightened.

Kairo whispered:

“Don’t interfere with my city.”

Zenith smiled wider.

“Oh?”

Then he spoke softly.

“You still think it is yours.”

---

SCENE 6 — REIKA FEELS IT

Back at the estate.

Reika collapsed slightly.

Akari rushed to her.

“Reika?!”

Reika held her chest.

Her eye burning violently.

“…He’s fighting.”

Kairo’s resonance echoed through her body.

But something else echoed too.

Something underneath it.

A second presence.

Far away.

Watching the same battle.

Mikaboshi’s influence stirred.

For the first time—

Reika heard two voices at once.

One familiar.

One ancient.

And both said nothing.

But meant everything.

END OF EPISODE 5
